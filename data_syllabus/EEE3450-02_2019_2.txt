Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of the course EEE3450 Communications Networks is to 
study networking technologies, protocols, and the architectural 
design of modern networks. The course contents include the 
Internet, IPv4, IPv6, TCP, UDP, MPLS, Flow & Error Control, 
ARQ, Routing, Ethernet, Wi-Fi,  Security, Blockchain, 
Cloud Computing (MEC, Fog, Cloudlet), Big Data systems 
(Hadoop, Spark), Video Streaming, MPEG-DASH, H.264, 
YouTube, Skype, 4G & 5G mobile communications.

Syllabus_English
##
Dr. Jong-Moon Chung is a professor in the School of 
Electrical & Electronic Engineering at Yonsei University. 
The goal of the course EEE3450 Communications Networks is to 
study networking technologies, protocols, and the architectural 
design of modern networks. The course contents include the 
Internet, IPv4, IPv6, TCP, UDP, MPLS, Flow & Error Control, 
Routing, Ethernet, Wi-Fi, Network Security, Blockchains, 
Cloud Computing (SaaS, PaaS, IaaS, MEC, Fog, Cloudlet), 
Big Data systems (Hadoop, Spark), MPEG-DASH, H.264, YouTube, 
Skype, 4G & 5G mobile communications. The class web site is 
http://web.yonsei.ac.kr/jmc/Lectures.htm (must use your 
real name when registering)
