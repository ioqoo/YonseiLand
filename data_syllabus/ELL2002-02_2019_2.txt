##
COURSE DESCRIPTION: 

Speech & Debate is a performance-based course that will give you the opportunity to develop and strengthen skills in preparing and presenting public oral presentations for a variety of situations. Students will work on skills in organization, delivery, and persuasion, as well as sharpen your skills in critical listening. Depending on the oral performance, preparation will include most or all of the following: writing an outline and/or speech script, creating a visual aid, and practicing your speech prior to delivering it in front of your audience of peers. The majority of class time will be devoted to giving you speaking opportunities, as well as to listen to and evaluate your classmatesâ€™ speeches. 


COURSE GOALS: By the end of this course, you will learn how to:

1.  plan, prepare, and deliver speeches that inform, persuade, or fulfill the needs of a
special occasion
2.  use presentation aids to enhance your speeches
3.  conduct meaningful research on an issue
4.  analyze your audience and design speeches to reflect your analysis
5.  evaluate and critic speeches given by your peers
6.  construct a clear, logical argument that is supported by evidence
7.  recognize and engage in both sides of an argument
8.  analyze and critique spoken arguments for flaws in reasoning or evidence
