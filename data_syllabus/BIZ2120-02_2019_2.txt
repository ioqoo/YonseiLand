Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to introduce undergraduate students to the fundamental principles and concepts of marketing and to provide them with a structure for applying marketing in managerial decision making. Through a combination of lecture, real world case discussion, and marketing planning, students will acquire abilities to: 

- Understand the strategic role and importance of marketing
- Understand fundamental marketing concepts and apply them in managerial decision making
- Identify and address the key decisions facing marketing managers. 
- Practice the process of analyzing a marketing situation or opportunity, formulating market strategy, and developing and implementing a marketing plan.
- Effectively work in a team and develop a professional and business-like attitude similar to that needed to operate in a real-world environment.
