Syllabus_Korean
##
The objective of the course is to help students to understand basic concepts 
of biology. Emphasis is on basic biochemistry, cell biology and genetics. The 
course will be a foundation for higher-level biology courses. In addition to 
the lecture, the students will have a chance to learn basic biological 
techniques in lab class.
