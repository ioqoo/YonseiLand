Syllabus_Korean
##
This course introduces students to the principles of foodservice systems. It discusses the basic theories of foodservice management and operations, including menu, production, safety, personnel, human resources, financial management, and technology.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course discusses the fundamental theories and practices as to qualifications, knowledge, techniques, and personality to become competent managers for non-commercial foodservice operations. Specifically, the course deals with management processes, human resource management, marketing of the non-commercial food service operations, and the recent trend in the industry.
