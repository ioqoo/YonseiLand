Syllabus_Korean
##
1) 상업공간의 디자인 변화 방향을 조사, 분석하여 추후 방향에 대해 알아보고자 한
다.
2) 실무에서 적용되는 디자인 단계에 기초하여, 실제 디자인 프로젝트를 수행하고자 
한다.
3) 다양한 분야와의 협업을 통한 공간디자인 방향을 살펴보고, 이를 실제 디자인에 적
용해 보고자 한다.

Syllabus_English
##
This class provides a broad perspective of commercial space design. There are 
three steps to explore commercial space design in this class. First, students 
research and analyse precedents of commercial space and have their own 
perspectives on design trends. The second step is to design a commercial space 
according to the design process : concept design, schematic design and design 
development. Finally, in the process of developing design projects, they survey 
many cases to design in the cooperation with various design departments. 
Through this task, they find creative inspiration and apply to their projects.
