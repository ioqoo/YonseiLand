Syllabus_Korean
##
This course introduces the principles in marketing and explores the practical 
applications of marketing and themes unique to the hotel and food service 
industry. Students will analyze customers, competitors and the business 
environment, and then develop a marketing plan. By the end of the course, 
students will develop a comprehensive strategy for marketing a company or an 
operation in the hospitality and tourism industry. 

COURSE OBJECTIVES
After this course, students will be able to achieve the following course objectives:
1. To utilize the language of marketing
2. To apply marketing principles to the hospitality and tourism industry
3. To discuss how the internet can aid in making marketing decisions and help 
market businesses.
4. To identify sources of marketing information. 
5. To develop sound marketing strategies
