Syllabus_Korean
##
Yonsei RC 101 is designed to help first year UIC students make the successful 
transition from high school to college. Main topics are 

- Orient students to UIC and Yonsei academic community
- Familiarize students with strategies and support services to ensure academic 
success
- Establish the values of academic integrity
- Introduce students to the campus culture, history and mission
- Connect students with faculty and fellow students
- Assist students in setting academic and career goals
