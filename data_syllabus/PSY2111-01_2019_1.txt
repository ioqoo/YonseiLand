Syllabus_Korean
##
Everyday we open our eyes and use vision to guide our eyes, action and understanding about the world. Vision is critical for human survival and daily interactions with the world. This course is a survey course to introduce students to important questions of vision and how current research uses visual system as a model to gain insight into the human mind and the brain. The goal is to introduce students with interesting questions about the vision, human mind and the brain. No pre-requisites.
