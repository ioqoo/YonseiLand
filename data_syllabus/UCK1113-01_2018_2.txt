Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed for students who want to improve their English 
reading comprehension and vocabulary together with speaking into a more 
advanced level using articles 
about current affairs.
 Course objectives include: enhancing English reading comprehension through an 
increased ability to analyze reading passages; identifying main ideas in 
reading passages; obtaining a better undertanding of human society through 
various cultures;improving listening with video news clips. In addition, 
students  will also be able to utilize useful expressions in real-world 
communication.
