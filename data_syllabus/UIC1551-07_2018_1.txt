Syllabus_Korean
##
This course surveys basic historical concepts, events and figures in Japanese history, by focusing on the history of modern Japan from 1600 to 1945.  Tracing major political, economic, social and cultural transformations in Japanese modern history, it examines the historical development of several institutions laying underneath such changes as a social foundation.  In particular, by analyzing Japanese history in relation to the context of world history, it aims to help students have a comprehensive understanding of international problems Japan faces in the present.
