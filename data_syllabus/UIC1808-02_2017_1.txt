Syllabus_Korean
##
Emphasis is placed on the fundamentals of listening, speaking, reading, and writing.  In addition, students will be exposed to everyday life contexts (language, culture, etc) likely to be encountered in contemporary Korean society society.   

At the end of the course, students will be able to: 
- understand the main ideas and supporting ideas when people talk about familiar topics.
- carry on a conversation on a variety of topics that are familiar to them. 
- understand short, simple readings on familiar topics.write a series of simple sentences on topics of interest.
