Syllabus_Korean
##
This course introduces the principles of production system, its the mechanism, 
and application of the principles to real practices. The course involves 
intensive laboratory experiments through which students can understand the 
basic factory physics and mechanism of production systems on their own hands. 
Real manufacturing cases are sought by all students and presented in class.
