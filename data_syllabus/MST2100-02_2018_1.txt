##
본수업에서는 물리와 화학을 바탕으로 재료의 microstructure, chemical composition, properties, synthesis, performance 사이의 상관관계를 분석하며 신소재공학에서 다루는 전반적인 engineering materials의 특성과 응용에 대해 강의함.

Syllabus_English
##
Materials Science and Engineering (MSE) aims to develop innovative and 
futuristic materials in the fields of metals, alloys, ceramics, polymers, 
biomaterials, and semiconductors, based on the principles of basic sciences 
and engineering. This course provides an overview of fundamental knowledge and 
industrial applications, which is a prerequisite for undertaking advanced MSE 
courses.
