Syllabus_English
##
1. The title of this class is "Educational Leadership." As is often the case with both general organizations and educational organizations, leadership is a single most critical factor for organizational development, improvement, or even success. Therefore, it is necessary to learn and understand what leaderhip is and why it is important in running an organization. 

2. Three goals of this class are 1) learning about traditional leadership theories, 2) learning about diverse concepts, models, and theories about leaderships in Educational organizations,3) applying what students learn to actual cases.

3. In a variety of ways inclusing lecture, discussion, mini case analysis, presentation, students may learn and understand the concepts, models, and theories of leadership thoroughly and have enough chance to apply what they learn to practice.
