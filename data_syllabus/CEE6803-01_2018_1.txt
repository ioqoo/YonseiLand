Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to introduce students methodologies for financing 
infrastructure projects. Upon successful completion of this 
course students should be able to understand:
 the nature of project finance
          differnt parties involved in project finance 
 risks in project finance
          project finance valuation
          project finance contracts
 different types of public private partnership
          real-options analysis
