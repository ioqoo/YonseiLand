Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic College English I an integrated, two-credit course designed to develop 
English skills for the students at the basic level. Students will engage in 
exercises to build the four language skills: reading, writing, listening, and 
speaking. Students will enhace ther pronunciation of sounds and conversational 
techniques as well as improving the listening and understanding of spoken 
English. They wil also understand and integrate basic reading and writing 
skills.

##
This course will enable students to effectively integrate basic strategies nad 
four skills to improve English proficiency.  Students will be able to clearly 
identify parts of a sentence and recognize meaningful units through reading 
and listening practices.  Through extensive exercises, students will also 
develop their writing and speaking skills at the sentence level.
