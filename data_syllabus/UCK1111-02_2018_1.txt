Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will empower students to improve their English fluency via a variety of methods such as "speed dating", self-recorded videos, reflective assignments, group projects, and class sessions based on the Toastmasters International club meeting model.  Subject matter will vary widely based on professor`s and students` collective interests.  Students will be encouraged to speak with authenticity and challenged to step outside their respective comfort zones.
