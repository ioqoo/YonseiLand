Syllabus_Korean
##
By the end of the semester, students will be familiar and conversant in the 
techniques of creative writing in two genres.
The first half of the class will introdice students to examining literature 
from a writer`s perspective in both fiction and poetry. The second half of the 
course will be writing workshops where students submit their own writing for 
discussion among their peers.
