Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to explore blurred trajectories between culture and design in the disruptive age of the fourth industrial revolution. Looking through shifts of socio-cultural values, lifestyle, and market by demographic change and environmental circumstances, design trends and forecast will be reviewed. By understanding and interconnecting keywords from culture and design trends, students will discuss immersive and interdisciplinary role of design.
