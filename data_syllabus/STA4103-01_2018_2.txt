Syllabus_English
##
This course is to introduce the concept and technique of data mining methods. 
This course will require the knowledge of intermediate statistics and 
regression. There will be a exam, several homework assignments, and a project. 
A project team will be organized during the lecture. There will a project 
presentation by each team.
