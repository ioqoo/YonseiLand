Syllabus_Korean
##
This class aims to deal with basic concepts, fundamental principles, ethics, 
history, and roles required for performing research projects as engineers. 
Additionally, students will have a chance to learn leadership, communication 
skills, creative thinking, and problem solving techniques through carrying out 
chemical engineering-related team projects.In the 2nd PBL, all students will 
attend a project designing and fabricating an equipment, which is highly 
related to chemical engineering fields.
