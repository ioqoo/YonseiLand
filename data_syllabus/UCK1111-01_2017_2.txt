Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will empower students to improve their English fluency via group projects, discussion, debate, presentations, and other speaking exercises and activities.  Though the focus is on speaking skills, students will engage in various aspects of language acquisition, including pronunciation, intonation, idioms, colloquialisms, and humor.  Subject matter will vary widely based on professor`s and students` collective interests, to maximize personal growth; therefore, English fluency is both a means and an end.
