Syllabus_Korean
##
This course investigates the value of fitness in daily life, examines personal 
fitness levels, and develops the ability to plan an individualized fitness 
program and diet.  Emphasis is placed on the personal development of fitness, 
with a goal to improve levels of fitness during the course.  The personal 
fitness program will incorporate the health related components of physical 
fitness.
