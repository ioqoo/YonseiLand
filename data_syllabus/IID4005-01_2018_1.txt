Syllabus_Korean
##
This class is extension of Photography 1, where you will have a whole semester to find a single project and work throughout the semester. It involves intense class critique of your work. Students are required to bring each week, printed picture of their work for discussion. We will also study discuss other artists each week. (students will pick artists  for discussion.)
##
Syllabus_English
##
Photography II class  is designed to continue exploring photography  as a tool to express ones aesthetic sensibility. Unlike photo I class, each students will work on their own project for the whole semester. Each week, students work will be discuss in depth. The whole purpose of  this critical thinking process will nurture your sensibility as an artist. At the end of the semester, students are expected to produce solid body of work in prints, or books, or in video format.
