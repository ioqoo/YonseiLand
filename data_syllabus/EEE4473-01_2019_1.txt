Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is composed of various practice programs to understand SoC design 
methodology and learn the advanced SoC design, how to optimize the target SoC, 
embedded S/W development methodologies for SoC, actual design techniques. 
Thus, this course trains the system architect professionals that can 
understand both H/W for the open ended problem and S/W for the capstone at the 
level of system through learning how to HW / SW integration.
