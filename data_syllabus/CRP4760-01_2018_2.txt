Syllabus_Korean
##
This course, Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS), applies to 
transportation systems that involve integrated applications of advanced 
surveillance, communications, computer, display and control process 
technologies, both in the vehicle and on the highway.  Various theory and 
design skills will be presented in the course.
The primary objective of this course is to provide the design abilility for 
overcoming various existing environmental constraints. To accomplish this 
objective, the students should solve the problems associated with safety, 
economics, ethics, etc. The study site is Yonsei Univerrsity and 
surrrounding arterials.
