Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides a basic, but comprehensive understanding of the economic, political, and cultural environments within which international business takes place. After completing this course, students will (1) understand the importance of ethics and corporate social responsibility in doing international business, (2) acquire the fundamental knowledge and analytical skills for conducting business across borders, and (3) gain a broader perspective on the global marketplace.
