Syllabus_Korean
##
새로운 교육환경인 대학생활을 경험하는 외국인 또는 12년 전교육 과정 해외 이수자 
학생들이 성공적으로 대학사회로 적응할 수 있도록 문화적응 과정을 이론적으로 설명
하고 문화 간의 커뮤니케이션이 어떻게 이루어지고 한국인들의 커뮤니테이션 방식을 
소개함으로 연세 공동체의 구성원으로 어울릴 수 있는 기반을 제공한다.

Many foreign students experience difficulties in communicating with Korean 
students and faculty members. How good their Korean abilities are, effective 
communication is hard to be achieved because it involves understanding of 
cultural context and nonverbal language. In this course, students are 
introduced to Korean culture specific communication environment and Korean 
nonverbal language.
##
Syllabus_English
##
For students who are from other countries around the world, it can be a very 
intimidating experience to adjust to a new cultural and educational 
environment. In order to make this less intimidating, it is critical to 
understand what obstacles are there to be overcome. Through this course, 
students are taught what culture shock is and how to deal with it. Further, 
various types of communication patterns that are unfamiliar with foreigners 
are observed.
