Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is for freshmen or sophomores of the School of Integrated 

Technology to do individual research. The students shall 

meet professors whose research areas are related to the interests of the 

students and shall decide the individual research topics and the research 

advisors.

##
Syllabus in English 

This course is for undergraduate students (freshmen and sophomores) to do 

individual research. The students shall meet professors whose research areas 

are related to the interests of the students and shall decide the individual 

research topics and the research advisors.
