Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed for UIC students who have no or very limited knowledge of Japanese. The class emphasizes the development of basic communication skills through intensive exercise and practice of sample patterns of dialogue and basic grammar. The focus is on speaking in everyday rituals and transactions along with reading the kana letters, pronunciation and basic sentence structures.
