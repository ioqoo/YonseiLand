Syllabus_Korean
##
This class will deal with the fundamental principles and techniques of 
quantitative/qualitative analytical measurements including data analysis, 
statistics, chemical equilibrium, and instrumental analysis, all of which are 
highly required in the field of chemical and biomedical 
industries/laboratories.
