Syllabus_Korean
##
목표:
반도체를 이해하고 어떻게 transistor가 작동하는 지를 user-friendly interface 
material을 통해 공부하고, 이 transistor들이 어떻게 basic CMOS logic과 memory 
circuit을 구성하는지를 알아본다.

개요:
- Silicon의 전기적 물성
_ PN junction
- MOS capacitor
_ MOSFET
- BJT
- Small Circuit
##
Syllabus_English
##
Target audience: 
Junior, senior undergraduate and interested students

Objective:
This course will provide a fundamental understanding of semiconductor devices, 
focusing on the semiconductor and how transistor works, in detail using 
user-friendly interface materials. Covered topics will be helpful to provide a 
strong intuitive and analytical foundation for dealing with modern solid state 
devices.

Course description:
1. Electronic properties of Silicon
2. The PN junction
3. The MOS Capacitor
4. The MOSFET
5. Small circuits

Prerequisites:
Physical electronics or equivalent classes related to solid state 
physics

Text and reading materials:
Lecture note
Ben G. Streetman and Sanjay Banerjee, Solid State Electronic Devices 5th 
edition, Prentice Hall (2000).
R. Pierret, Semiconductor Device Fundamentals, Addison Wesley (1996).
