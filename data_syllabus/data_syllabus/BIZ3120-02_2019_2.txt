Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is an introduction to asset pricing and capital markets, and its objective is to understand the theoretical foundations and practical applications of investment decision making. The fundamental economic concepts, financial theory and other major topics covered in the course include: the law of one price and arbitrage, risk and risk aversion, the risk-return trade-off, the concept of diversification and the efficient frontier, Capital Asset Pricing Model, Arbitrage Pricing Theory, the notion of market efficiency and empirical evidence on security returns, the yield curve and forward rates, portfolio performance evaluation, and applications of portfolio management.
