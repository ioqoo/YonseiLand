Syllabus_Korean
##
본 강의는 지식기반경제시대에 조직경쟁력의 핵심원천이라 할 수 있는 인적자원을 효
과적으로 관리하는 방안에 대해 다룬다. 구체적으로 본 강의에서는 인적자원관리의 
기본방향과 원칙, 인적자원의 전략적 관리방안, 직무분석 및 직무설계, 인력모집과 
선발, 성과관리 및 직무수행 평가, 보상관리 등에 대해 배운다.

본 수업에서는 다음의 2 Learning Goals을 추구한다.
1. 창의력(creativity) 향상
2. 의사소통, 의견개진 역량(communication skill) 향상

Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to provide students with the basic concepts and 
theories of managing people and their applications in contemporary 
organizations. We adopt the perspectives of both general and HR managers. We 
study the environment of HRM, key HRM functions (e.g., recruitment, 
performance management, compensation), characteristics of effective HRM 
systems (e.g., strategic alignment of HRM functions), and various other 
important topics. We will not delve into many technical details of issues 
relevant only to HR professionals (e.g., all the specifics of job evaluation 
or the psychometric aspects of test validation). In delivering those issues, 
diverse company cases and expert perspectives will be presented and discussed.

Upon completion of this course, the following Learning Goals will be fostered;
1. Communication skills
2. Creativity
