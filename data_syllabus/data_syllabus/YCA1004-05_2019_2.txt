Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to introduce Christianity to undergraduates 
who register in Yonsei University established with a Christian spirit, i.e., 
Truth and Freedom (John 8:32), and to enable them to experience Christian
services with thoughtful and meaningful sermons/speeches, music, performances, 
and special programs.

*This Chapel doesn`t use a traditional worship style like Benediction.
