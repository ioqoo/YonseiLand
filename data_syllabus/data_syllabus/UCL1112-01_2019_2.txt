Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic skills and rules of table tennis will be introduced. Throughout the 
semester, you`ll learn the proper grip for your style of play, the basic 
strokes, and the correct way to apply spin to the ball.
Finally you will learn how to play and enjoy table tennis game.
