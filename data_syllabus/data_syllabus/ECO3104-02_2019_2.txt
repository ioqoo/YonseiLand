Syllabus_English
##
This course deals with the basic theory for regression analysis.  The topics 
covered are: least squares estimation, Gauss-Markov theorem, test for 
regression coefficients, heteroskedasticity, autocorrelation, 
multicollinearity, endogeneity, and etc.  It also introduces some real world 
applications.
