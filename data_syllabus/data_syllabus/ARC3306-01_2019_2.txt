Syllabus_English
##
This is an advanced level building construction course. “Fundamentals of Building Construction” is a prerequisite. The entire course is structured to help teams of students develop preliminary construction plans for unbuilt buildings or buildings built in foreign countries through lectures, guest lectures, site visits, and literature survey. Students will be asked to invite a technical advisor who can help them develop high-quality construction plans.
