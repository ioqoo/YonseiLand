Syllabus_English
##
This course is for students who have had one(1) semester of Russian at Yonsei University or its equivalent. This means the students have mastered the Russian alphabet, know the gender of nouns, understand what noun declention is and have mastered basic cases in singular, know the past and present tense of Russian basic verbs and can use tchem.  

There will be a placement test on the first day to determine if each student actually belongs in this level. Students whose first language is Russia or those who are from Russian-speaking countries may not take this course.
