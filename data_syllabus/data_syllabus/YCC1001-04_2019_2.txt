Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic College English I is an integrated, two-credit course designed to develop fundamental English
skills for students at the basic level. Student will engage in exercises to build the four core language
skills: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Students will enhance their pronunciation of sounds
and conversational techniques as well as improve their listening and understanding of spoken English.
They will also understand and integrate basic reading and writing skills.
Basic College English I will enable students to effectively integrate basic strategies and four skills to
improve English proficiency. Students will be able to clearly identify meaningful units, key words, and
major points through reading and listening practices. Through extensive exercises, students will also
develop their writing and speaking skills at the sentence level.
