Course Description: Basic College English II is an integrated, two-credit course designed to build on the fundamental skills covered in Basic College English I. To further develop the four core language skills, students will be exposed to a variety of texts: thematically-related reading and writing materials and speaking-conducive listening clips. The extensive, challenging practices will improve skills that are essential to academic environment as well as real-world communication.
