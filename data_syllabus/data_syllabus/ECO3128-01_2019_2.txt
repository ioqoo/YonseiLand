Syllabus_Korean
##
거시 및 금융시계열의 계량분석을 위한 이론과 응용.

개략적인 수업 주제 (수강생들의 관심에 따라 강의내용을 조정하겠음. 시간 제약으로 일부는 생략할 수 있음.)
1. 시계열분석 이론
2. Generalized Method of Moments (GMM)
3. Vector Autoregressive Models (VAR)
4. State Space Models (Kalman Filtering)
5. Unit Root and Cointegration
6. Autoregressive Conditional Heteroskedasticity (ARCH)

Syllabus_English
##
IMPORTANT: Students must have taken Econometrics I and will be assumed to be 
competent in calculus, linear algebra, and probability theory.

We will study basic topics in time series analysis that are particularly 
useful for macroeconomic as well as financial applications. Topics include 
ARMA models, GMM, VAR, Unit Root, and Cointegration. 

Evaluation will be based on two exams(40% and 50% respectively), and class 
participation and homework(10%).
