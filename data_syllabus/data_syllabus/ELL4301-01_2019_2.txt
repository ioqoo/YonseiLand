Syllabus_Korean
##
This course overviews an introduction to semantics with some of the
central ideas in the field. It starts with a general introduction to the
concept of meaning and go further on to some of the important issues in
semantics, such as lexical relations, sentence relations with situations and
participants, the role of context in semantic interpretation, and other
cognitive semantics.
