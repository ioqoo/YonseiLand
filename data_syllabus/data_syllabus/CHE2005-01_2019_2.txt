Syllabus_English
##
The role of chemistry on the development of nanotechnology will be studied. 
Unique experiences of nanoscience background, experimental obeservations and participations of state-of-the-art instruments (e.g. TEM, XRD, VSM, AFM, PL, Chromatography etc) and tour of world class Yonsei IBS Institute will comprise this course.
