Syllabus_Korean
##
We try to review the historical development of the big bang cosmology
and understand the basics of the big bang theory, dark matter, dark energy,
inflation, CMB.

Typically (but with flexibility) Mondays are for lectures and Wednesdays are 
for TA classes.
