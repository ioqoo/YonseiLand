##
Calculus is a branch of mathematics whose primary purpose is the study of 
motion and change. It is an indispensable tool of thought in almost every 
field of pure and applied science in physics, chemistry, biology, astronomy, 
geology, engineering, and even some of the social sciences. It also has many 
important uses in other parts of mathematics.
