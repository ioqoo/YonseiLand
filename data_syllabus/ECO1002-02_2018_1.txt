Syllabus_English
##
Economics is the social science studying the production, distribution and 
consumption of goods and services. It is a complex social science that spans 
from mathematics to psychology. At its most basic, however, economics 
considers how a society provides for its needs. Its most basic need is 
survival. These all have different emphases on certain aspects of economics.
