Syllabus_Korean
##
**This syllabus will be further updated by the first lecture.**

This module aims to provide students with knowledge and research tools necessary to better understand governing phenomenon of today.  Students will be encouraged to learn how to use theoretical lenses with a view to examining government’s governing behaviour.
This module is designed to achieve its aim by providing the following knowledge and opportunities: 1) qualitative methodologies; 2) theories of governance, policy instruments, and mayoral leadership; and 3) analytic approaches.
This lecture involves conveying cutting-edge knowledge and discussing participants’ own views on cases and theories.
