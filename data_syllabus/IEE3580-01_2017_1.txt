Course Description
This course introduces the Korean Government and Policy. Course participants will brifely study the history of the Korean Government and Korean Public policies. In order to help your understanding, this course emphasizes comparison of the Korean government and others (e.g., US government or others). So, students are expected to introduce their governemnt systems and policies with colleagues. 

Learning Objectives 
Upon successful completion of this course, the student will be expected to:
1. Discuss the foundations and base of the Korean government and policies. 
2. Describe why and how the Korean governemnt and polices are different from others.
