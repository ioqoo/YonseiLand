Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to not only introduce the basic principle and general knowledge of biology that involves the scientific study of living organisms but also to bring up intellectual curiosity of students. In the course, students will grasp general outlines from the basic form and function of unsophisticated organisms such as viruses to complex human beings. Students will be introduced the basic concepts and perspectives of biology and acquire a common vocabulary useful to understand biology (e.g. standard part, chassis, etc.)
