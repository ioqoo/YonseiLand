Syllabus_Korean
##
This course integrates the theoretical and practical issues concerning the 
operation of the marketing discipline. More specifically the class will: 1. 
review the existing conceptual and managerial issues concerning product, 
price, distribution and promotion. 2. help students develop creative mindsets 
and skills to approach a variety of marketing problems and formulate 
appropriate marketing strategies and implementation. 3. as a Flipped course, encourage/force 
students to participate in discussions related to marketing problems via 
methods such as case studies and simulation exercises. 4. engage in a new 
pedagogical method of interactive learning via the use of online education.
5. to instill an ethical philosophy in the conceptualization and deployment of 
marketing.
