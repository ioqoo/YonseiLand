Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic concepts in the analysis of survival data will be introduced. Emphasis 
is given to learning models, methods, theories, and applications in survival 
analysis for gaining a broader and deeper understanding of the subject. Topics 
of discussion include basic concepts including survival models, censoring and 
truncation, nonparametric estimation of survival functions, comparison of 
survival functions for different treatment groups, parametric and 
nonparametric regression models, mathematical and graphical methods for 
assessing goodness of fit, analysis of multivariate failure time data, 
competing risks analysis and 
cohort-sampling designs.
