Syllabus_English
##
The Consciousness-only school has founded Ālaya-vijñāna theory, understanding 
widely functions of consciousness, and has proved organically roles and  
functions of Ālaya-vijñāna. One of features in the texts of the Consciousness-
only school is that they named Ālaya-vijñāna as the seed of consciousness(一切種
子識) in functional aspect, and adopted the seed theory(種子說) actively.
