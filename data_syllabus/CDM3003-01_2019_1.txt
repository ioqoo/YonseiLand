Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to cultivate a student`s ability to develop and prototype design ideas based on advanced digital technologies.
In this class, students basically learn how to use Rhino SW and the 3D FDM(Fused deposition modeling) technology as a new manufacturing media. They will also experience a new prototyping process such as VR(Virtual Reality)-based modeling and sculpting for the 3D printing process.
