Syllabus_Korean
##
The students who take this course are required to meet with high school 
students, middle school students or elementary school students in Yeon Su Gu 
or 
Nam Dong Gu area on a regular basis during the semester and help with their 
foreign language development. By doing so, the participating students will 
have 
an opportunity to serve students who need extra training with their foreign 
languages. Please sign up for insurance program offered to all students who 
take HE1 at www.1365.go.kr. You can get an official copy of your activity 
report once you are registered there.
Depending on where you are assigned to, mentoring hours may vary. You will be 
randomly assigned the school where you mentor.
