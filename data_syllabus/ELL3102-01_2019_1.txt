Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to survey key concepts and current issues in the field of English morphology, focusing on how English words are structured, generated and understood. Upon successful completion of this course, you should be able to:

* Find patterns from language data
* Formulate rules and identify/discover constraints that explain observed data
* Critically evaluate current research in linguistics
