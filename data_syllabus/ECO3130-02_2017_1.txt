Syllabus_Korean
##
This course covers international finance, including topics intermediate theoretical models, policy issues, and interpretations of open macroeconomic 
phenomenon. It is taught at the intermediate level. The purpose of this course is to train students to think systematically about the current state of the 
economy in terms of international finance, and to evaluate the open macroeconomic environment in which economic decisions are made.
