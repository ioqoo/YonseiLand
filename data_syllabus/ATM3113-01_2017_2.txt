Syllabus_English
##
Aims at learning some basic statistical concept and tools that are needed to 

understand and present data in atmospheric science fields, such as numerical 

and graphical summary measures, exploratory techniques, probability 

distributions, and parametric tests.



Grading will be based on midterm and final exams (80%) and homework 

assignments (20%)
