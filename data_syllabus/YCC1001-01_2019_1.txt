Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this Basic English course is to improve students’ English knowledge, so that they are able to express their own thoughts and feelings with confidence.   This is a four skills class: speaking, listening, reading and writing.  

Goals
Students improve their English knowledge
Students develop confidence speaking in English
Students expand their understanding of various topics
