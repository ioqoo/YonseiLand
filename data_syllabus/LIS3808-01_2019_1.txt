Syllabus_English

Goal
This course deals with theories and practices in constructing the academic web sites. 
It improves the ability of web contents planning and technical skills in using 
various softwares for developing web sites.

Grading
Exam(Mid-term, Final): 30%
Team project: 30% 
Class participation and attendance: 10%
