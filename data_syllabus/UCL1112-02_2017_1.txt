Syllabus_Korean
##
This class is designed to introduce students the fundamentals of table tennis,
specific rules and strategies to enjoy the game. In addition, the students
will learn essential team playing skills and etiquette through doubles games.
This course will enable students to enjoy table tennis as a recreational
lifetime activity.
