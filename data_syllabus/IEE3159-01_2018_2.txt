Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is put together to provide students with a thorough understanding of the function, structure and mechanism of money, banking system and financial markets. It will allow students to examine not ony the origins and nature of money, but also financial institutions and markets that have evolved over time and also help students to develop an appreciaion for important concepts and theories in economics, from interest rates and central banking to stocks and bonds.
