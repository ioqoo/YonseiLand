Syllabus_Korean
##
본 강의에서는 자연과학, 인문과학, 경영, 경제,공학 등의 기본이 되는 벡터 공간의

Syllabus_English
##
This course presents the fundamentals of linear algebra covering reviews of 
systems of linear equations, determinants, vector spaces and new topics 
including inner product spaces, linear transformations, eigenvalues and 
eigenvectors. It also gives and elementary treatments of its applications.

Grade: 
First Midterm 90pts, Final 1110pts

Text: 
Contemporary linear algebra by Howard Anton, Robert C. Busby
