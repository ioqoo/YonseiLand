Syllabus_Korean
##
By the end of the class, you should be better able to evaluate research, read 
a paper from the primary literature, and should have a better understanding of 
how research in developmental cognitive psychology is conducted. You should 
also be familiar with some of the controversies and findings in the area of 
cognitve development, as well as the techniques used to adress these issues.
