Syllabus_Korean
##
In order to become a design leader, it is vital to consider holistically business elements from the beginning. Students will learn, experience, and create marketing strategy from a design perspective. The course focuses on business environment analysis, STP strategy, brand strategy, trend research, marketing mix, meeting real customers and users, and creating a marketing plan. This is similar to Design Business (DSN4120).
