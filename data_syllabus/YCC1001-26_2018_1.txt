Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic English is an integrated course 
designed to improve beginner to pre intermediate reading,writing listening and speaking  
competencies essential to successfully communicate in a future professional or academic environment. 
Students will be exposed to authentic listening materials and will learn to 
communicate clearly and effectively in a group setting. Students will become 
active speakers and strategic listeners.
