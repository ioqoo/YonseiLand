Syllabus_English
##
The course aims to promote peace regarding the comfort women issue.

There are various activities and institutions related to this issue, such as 
the Wednesday peace demonstration and the War and Women`s Human Rights Museum. 
Yonseians interested in institutional management, organizing demonstrations, 
disseminating information through various medias, etc. should support it.

The volunteers for promoting peace will work with staff at the office located 
in Mapo-gu. The volunteers will be assigned as assistants for conducting 
regular peace demonstrations in front of the Japanese Embassy, as well as 
managing the War and Women`s Human Rights Museum, etc.
