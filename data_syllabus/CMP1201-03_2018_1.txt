Syllabus_Korean
##

음악적 이해를 돕게 할 예정입니다.

Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to improve the basic skills of musicianship through 
focusing on sight singing and dictating rhythm patterns, intervals, scales, 
modes, chords, and simple melodies. Due to the nature of the course’s skill-
based workshop approach, drills and quizes are essential for mastery of the 
material. As a result, students will be graded on attendance, quizes and 
tests. 
Excercises will be excerpted from well-known classical pieces, so that the 
course contents can actually help students for their overall understanding of 
music.
