Syllabus_Korean
##
From the steam engines, railway, canals, telephones, telegraphs, gramophones, sewing machines, bicycles, typewriters to the camera, the nineteenth century in Victorian Britain was a period of enormous technological upheaval and change.In this course, we will examine fiction that feature women telegraph operators, photographers, and typists who were workers and users of new inventions that were patented in the Victorian era.
