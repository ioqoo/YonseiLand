Syllabus_Korean
##
Intermediate Korean (2) is as a continuation of Intermediate Korean (1), is to 

equip students with communicative skills in reading, speaking, listening and 

writing with more complex structures in Korean. Students are expected to be 

able to command a lengthy dialogue regarding various topics such as birthday 

party, hair style and fashion, hobbies, traditional holidays, marriage, jobs 

and etc. Classroom activities are focused to impart students an understanding 

of Korean culture and heritage, so it is to help students attain language 

proficiency within the context of the Korean culture.
