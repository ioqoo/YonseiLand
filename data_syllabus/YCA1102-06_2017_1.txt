Syllabus_English
##
This class will focus on three objectives.
1. Critical and appreciative analysis of the history and culture of Christianity
2. Critical and appreciative analysis of the issues of post -modern society in the perspective of Christian tradition and ideals
3. Critical and appreciative understanding of self and develop self-identity
Class evaluation is based on attendance, class participation,small group activities, book report, final paper.
