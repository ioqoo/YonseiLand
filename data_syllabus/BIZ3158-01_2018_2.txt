Syllabus_Korean
##
The ability to communicate is fundalmental in your success as a member of any 
busines, and is key to being identified as a potential leader. This course 
will focus on the study and practice of business communication: written, oral, 
and interpersonal. Students will develop listening skills, create and deliver 
effective presentations; build interpersonal communication in teams; give and 
receive feedback; and learn to communicate across cultures.

By the end of this course you will learn how to do the following:

-Competently handle business communication situations requiring such
qualities as tactfulness, firmness, persuasiveness, and ethical behavior.
-Identify critical differences communication in domestic, global, and 
multicultural business operations
-Effectively express opinions and discuss issues in a professional manner.
-Operate in team-based projects in a competent, cooperative and 
constructive manner.
-Give oral presentations that are well-organized, clear, and understandable; 
-Analyze communication situations and audiences to make choices about
the most effective and efficient way to communicate and deliver messages
-Provide feedback, accept feedback, and use feedback to improve
communication skills 
-Write business reports containing logical analysis and valid conclusions
