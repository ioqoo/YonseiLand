Syllabus_Korean
##
ACE 1 is an integrated, two-credit course designed to broaden the academic 
listening and speaking skills. The course exposes students to listening and 
speaking strategies for successful communication in academic environments. 

Students will engage in discussions, debates, speeches, and presentations. 
They will also be required to take notes for extended listenings.  Also, 
students will develop cooperative learning habits by working collaboratively 
in small groups. Students will also expected work outside of class.
