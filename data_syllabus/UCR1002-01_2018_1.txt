Syllabus_Korean
##
“2-D Art: Foundation and Development” is for non-art major students and 
beginners to experience the process of art-making: students will learn how to
explore their own concepts through projects designed to reinforce the elements 
and principles of art and design. Various art materials will be used 
throughout the curriculum and each project is assigned with a different 
medium; projects will include materials such as ink pen, colored pencil, watercolor,
and poster color.
