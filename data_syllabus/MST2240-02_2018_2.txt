Syllabus_English
##
-Physical understanding and general quantification of phase equilibria, one of the fundamental principles of material science through the consideration for macroscopic quantification on the effect of heat, mechanical work, and chemical compositions on the stability of materials.
-Understanding the basic theories involved with chemical reactions in solutions through phase equilibria and microscopic interatomic interactions.
