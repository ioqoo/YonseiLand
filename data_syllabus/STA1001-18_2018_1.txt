Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will provide an introduction to probability and statistics with a view toward applications. It includes mathematical models for random  phenomena, random variables, expectation, and common discrete & continuous distribution with applications. It also covers laws of large numbers, the central limit theorem, and basic techniques of inferential statistics. 
Students are expected to be familiar with statistical thinking and the basic concepts of descriptive statistics, probability distribution, and inferential statistics through this course.
