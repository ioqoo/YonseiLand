Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this course is to learn principles, process, and methods of information design and interaction design through design projects. Although this is a project-based course, you will expect to study key concepts and relevant topics in both fields. In addition, you will have a chance to conduct design research, employ design methods in practice, and create design artifacts in response to the problem identified.
