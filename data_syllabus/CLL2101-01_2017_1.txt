##
중국어의 음운 체계, 문자 체계, 문법 체계의 분석 및 중국어사와 중국어 방언학의 연

Syllabus_English
##
This course is an introduction to Chinese as an object of linguistic analysis. 
Topics include nature and characteristics of the Chinese language, Chinese 
dialects, the development of Standard Chinese, phonological characteristics of 
modern Chinese, phonological history of the Chinese language, the history and 
development of Chinese writing system, and syntactic structure of the Chinese 
language.


Grades will be based on the following requirements.

- Attendance & participation 20%
_ Homework & Term paper 20% 
- Midterm 30%
- Final examination 30%
