Syllabus_English
##
IMPORTANT: Students must have taken Econometrics I and will be assumed to be 
competent in basic linear algebra as well as calculus.

We will study basic topics in time series analysis that are particularly 
useful for macroeconomic as well as financial applications. Topics include 
ARMA models, GMM, VAR, Unit Root, and Cointegration. 

Evaluation will be based on two exams(40% and 50% respectively), and class 
participation and homework(10%).
