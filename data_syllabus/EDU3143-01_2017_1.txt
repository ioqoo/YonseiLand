있는 `소셜미디어`에 대한 내용 강의 실시. 금요일은 `교재연구 및 활용`에 대한 강
의 실시
★수요일 교재는 무료 제공(대학생활의 에센스, SNS)
  금요일 교재는 개별 구입(교재의 이해와 활용, 교육과학사) 

1. Course Description
Overview of the theoretical frameworks and practices related to teaching 
materials & methods in Education Courses.

2. Course Objectives
By the conclusion of the course, students should be able to:
a. Explore some major concepts, characteristics, processes, and environmental 
factors related to teaching materials & methods.  
b. Analyze various issues regarding teaching method from the perspective of 
teaching materials & methods.
c. Analyze the various systems of teaching materials & methods.
