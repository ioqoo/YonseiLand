Syllabus_Korean
##
- Behavior and characteristics of steel structural systems
- Material properties of steel, Geometric properties of steel sections
- Calculation and Distribution of externally applied load
- Analysis of structural members and connections using Allowable Stress Design 
(ASD) method: beams, columns, beam-columns, tension members; shear and moment 
connections, welded and bolted connections.
- Tutorial sessions include problem-solving
##
Syllabus_English
##
Analysis of steel structures based on Allowable Stress Design (ASD) Method is 
discussed along with fundamentals in designing steel buildings. The course is 
consisted of the analysis of beams, columns, beam-columns, braces, and 
connections.
