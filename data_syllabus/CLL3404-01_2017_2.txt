##

Syllabus_English
##
The Hundred Schools of Thought (Chinese: 諸子百家; pinyin: zh?z? b?iji?) were philosophies and schools that flourished during the Spring and Autumn period and the Warring States period of ancient China.
This class is through reading the stories of The Hundred Schools of Thought, we suppose to understand the feature of them, to develop the reading comprehension of Chinese classical prose and also to get the wisdom of life.
For this we will read original texts in class and explore the famous phrases or keywords and debate about them all. 
The studying evaluation will be fixed according to attendance 30%,  class participation 30%, midterm examination 20%, final examination 20%.
