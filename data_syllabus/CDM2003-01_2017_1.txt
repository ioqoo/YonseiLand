Syllabus_Korean
##
Students understand the cultural differences between East and West with the examples of art, architecture and design. 
Students understand the systems of culture industries(art, music, film and game) and their practices.
##
Syllabus_English
##
In this course the cultural differences between East and West will be taught and their examples will be discussed. And the overall systems of culture industries such as art, music, film and game industry will be taught and the experts from these areas will give the lectures on their experiences.
