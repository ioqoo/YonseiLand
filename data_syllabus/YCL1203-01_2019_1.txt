Syllabus_Korean
##
생명경시 사조에 대한 대책: 현대 우리나라의 생명관, 생명의 역사와 존귀함 
생물의 역사는 생명 만들기의 역사: 무성생식과 유성생식은 생명을 만드는 두 가지 방법, 너무나 많은 종류의 생물들은 모두 번식을 한다. 
성의 이해: 성의 역사는 생물이 후손을 만들기 위한 전략 형성의 과정 
인간의 성: 우리나라의 성인식(성의 이중성, 성 터부시하기), 생명을 만드는 방식, 성 지식과 피임의 원리, 성과 건강, 미래의 성 전략

Syllabus_English
##
Life is priceless! Ironically, this great maxim seems to reflect that modern Koreans tend to cheapen their lives in many practical sides. Such alert urged us to iniate some responses. This was the primary reason why this course was designed. Another alert was come form our attitudes to sex that is directly related life. Actually this point is not well accepted and thus not widespread between people in our society. Therefore, it appears to be important to equip critical minds on sex. Furthermore, there have been no course of natural science with respect to these topics in our university. Based on these backgrounds altogether, this course was designed. Purpose of this courese ls to let students re-think value of life and human sex and gender at least partly in terms of biology. Covered by this course is definition of life, history of life, reproduction modes, sexual reproduction, and human sex and related practical things.
