Syllabus_Korean
##
통계학을 데이터 분석에 적용할 수 있는 이론지식과 실무능력을 습득한다. 데이터가 
주어졌을 때 적절한 분석기법을 익히고 이를 연습시간을 통해 실습한다.
##
Syllabus_English
##
The course is an introduction to the use and limitation of mathematical and 
statistical techniques in management, particularly in high technology 
contexts. Several statistical techniques are covered and examined for 
application in quantitative decision making. The main purpose of the course is 
to provide useful information and insights to support uses of statistics.
