Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides an introduction to recent trends in wearable electronics as stretchable free-form devices, including displays and smartphones, and the fundamental physics of soft, elastic materials.
(i) Recent trends in wearable electronics
(ii) Physics of soft, elastic materials for wearable electronic devices
(iii) Principles of their operation circuits
(iv) Packaging systems for wearable electronics
