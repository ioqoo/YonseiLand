Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a 4 skills course integrating listening, speaking, reading and writing. Students will examine Engineering challenges and possible solutions that will be a result of global warming. We will not be studying climate change per se, Examples of these challenges are sustainable reusable energy production, sustainable food production, housing design, transportations (both vehicles and infrastructure) and whatever else students can think of. Students will work in groups of diverse majors to find different perspectives and approaches to solutions.
