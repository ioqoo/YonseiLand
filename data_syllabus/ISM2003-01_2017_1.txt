Syllabus_Korean
##
Within International Studies and Political Science, the field of International Relations (IR) is focused on understanding and analyzing the interaction of states. The course will lay the groundwork for your intellectual participation in the disciplines discourses, past, present, and future. First will be an exploration of baseline concepts, principles, assumption, etc. Building on this, the course will turn to theory: The field has developed a significant body of theory in order to properly understand, conceptualize, and hypothesize about international political matters. This course introduces you to the core theoretical concepts and theories. In addition, critical questions will be raised and recent debates explored, and core discourses explored in order to link concepts and theories to practice.
