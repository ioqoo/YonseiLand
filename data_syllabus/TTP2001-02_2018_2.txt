Syllabus_English
##
1)Introductory understanding of individual subject areas in pedagogy; Basic 
understanding of various issues on education
2)Having free discussions on the nature of education and happiness
Who is a good person and what is the good, happy life? How can we achieve 
them? We may wonder if and how our education has responded to these questions. 
The ancient Greek thinkers` concern was with self-love. Self-love is taking 
care of the health of one`s soul. The healthy state of one`s soul is what 
constitutes virtue. How close is our education or alternative education to the 
education for "a life of virtue"?
