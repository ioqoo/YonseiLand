Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is to provide basic understandings about family studies, on which 
knowledge and concepts of family, family relations, family programs, and famiy 
policy could be developed. For this purpose, we will first learn definitions 
and concepts of family. Second, diverse family theories and studying methods 
will be considered. Lastly, contemporary family issues of Korean and 
international families will be discussed.
