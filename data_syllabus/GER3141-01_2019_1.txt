Syllabus_English
##
The course objective for students is to get prepared for the new exam of the 
GI. Students are supposed to get familar with the exam so. We will address all 
four areas of skills, i.e. Listening Comprehension, Speaking, Reading and 
Writing. Students who plan to take this class MUST be on level A2/B1!
