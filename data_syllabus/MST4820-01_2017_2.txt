Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will introduce the students to the integration of two of the most 
important disciplines of the 21st century, biotechnology and nanotechnology.
Special effort will be made to expose students to the basic principles, 
techniques, applications, and great prospects at the intersection of these two 
fields.
##
Syllabus_English
##
- Understanding biological molecules/materials & self-assembly process in 
biological system
- Self-assembly process in general
- Nanotechnology in general
- Colloid science
- Lessons from Biology: How they can be applied to nanotechnology.
