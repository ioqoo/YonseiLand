Syllabus_Korean
##
1. To understand the fundamentals of chemical engineering heat transfer, 
including various types of heat transfer mechanism.
2. To have an ability to identify and analyze each heat transfer system, and 
perform calculations for related situations. 
3. To have a strong theoretical background for heat transfer and to understand 
the analogy between heat and momentum transfer. 
4. To have an experience in modern computational and visualization algorithms, 
which may be very helpful when the students solve numerous heat transfer 
demonstrations and experiments. 
5. To function effectively on teams and to communicate effectively with others.
