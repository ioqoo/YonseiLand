Syllabus_Korean
##
이전 과정에서 습득한 기초조형 및 프로토타이핑 경험을 바탕으로, Background Research 및 Ideation 과정을 통해 설득력 있는 디자인 아이디어를 도출해내고 Idea Sketch 및 반복적인 Prototyping 과정을 통해 제품의 형태를 기능적, 심미적 관점에서 다듬고 발전시켜 학기말 최종 프리젠테이션과 과제전시를 통해 완성도 있는 디자인 결과물로 제시하는 것을 목표로 합니다.

Syllabus_English
##
The final goal of this course is developing abilities of implementing design ideas into tangible products on the basis of the basic formative trainings and prototyping skills acquired from the previous foundation courses, especially 제품디자인기초. Each student will design and present an IoT product at the end of this course through the design processes such as background research, ideation, idea sketch and iterative prototyping. Basic competency of 3D CAD applications such as Rhinoceros is required at the final prototyping stage.

Final grades will be determined as follows.
Midterm Exam : Project interim presentation : 30%
Final Exam : Final presentation and coursework exhibition : 30+20%
Attitude during the course : 10%
Attendance : 10%
