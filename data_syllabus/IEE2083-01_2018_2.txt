Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides a comprehensive introduction to Korean art and visual culture. Art works in diverse media, including murals, painting, sculpture, ceramics, calligraphy, and architecture, will be examined in their cultural and historical contexts. We will be investigating interrelationships between art, literature, religious philosophy, and politics. There will be some consideration of relationships in art between Korea and China and other cultural traditions.
