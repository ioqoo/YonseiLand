Syllabus_Korean
##
The aim of the course is to enable students to assess the opportunities and problems that managers in a wide range of organizations face as they attempt to use IT applications to add value to their businesses. It also aims to help students understand transformational changes in industries and in the society we are living in. These changes have strategic and social implications for businesses and people. Students will be able to assess the opportunities and risks for businesses, and discuss related social issues from a variety of stakeholder perspectives.  

At the end of the course, you will be able to: 
 
1. Explain basic concepts and technologies for IT/IS management and Digital Business;
2. Discuss organizational, strategic and social issues surrounding IT/IS and Digital Business; 
3. Analyze and evaluate uses of strategic IT/IS in practice.
