Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this course is to learn the basic concept of life science, including animal and plant cell structures and functions, inheritance, growth and development, division, differentiation, and metabolism. 
Major topics include the philosophy of science, scientific method, biochemical organization of life, cell biology, energy dynamics, and genetics.
This course will offer a comprehensive understanding of life sciences as well as a broad perspective on applicability of such knowledge in this field of study.
