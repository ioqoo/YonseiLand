Syllabus_Korean
##
The overall aim of this course is to help students develop a positive attitude and mindset towards English, so that they will no longer have fears or stress about upgrading their English language skills. 

The goals of this course are to help students:

- be more confident and effective when giving presentations;
- be more confident and fluent when participating in an academic or professional discussion;
- raise their listening comprehension of professional media and academic lectures;
- enlarge the vocabulary that they use for academic speaking; and 
- improve their attitude toward and enjoyment of developing their English language skills.
