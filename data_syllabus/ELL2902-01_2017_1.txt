Syllabus_Korean
##
As an introductory class to the field of interpreting, this course offers an 
overview of the basic elements that comprise effective interpreting/translation.
##
Syllabus_English
##
Students will be introduced to the activities and techniques used to improve 
interpreting skills, such as source text analysis, paraphrasing, sight 
translation, and note-taking/symbolization.
(This is a tentative on-line syllabus and is subject to change.)
