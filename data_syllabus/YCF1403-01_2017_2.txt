Syllabus_Korean
##
Please note. this is NOT a content based course. This course focuses mainly on mastering professional/ technical communication skills. This means that students are instructed in development of professional & technical documents and presentations of such material in various formats in interactive setting. 

(Preview: Definition of Tech Trends, Brochure, Manual, Product/Service Reviews, Crowd Funding Ideas, Start-up/ Venture Capital Business Proposal,Discussion of Tech Trends, Science & Tech Job Markets) 
 Literature source: MIT Technology Review and

By the end of this course, students should be able to speak and write at a high level of proficiency about a range of topics related to science and technology.
