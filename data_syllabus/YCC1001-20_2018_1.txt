Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic College English is an integrated, two-credit course designed to assist students in improving their English skills at the basic level. This course focuses on the four core language skills: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Students will be exposed to variety of texts, including conventional/unconventional materials like thematically-related reading and writing materials, as well as listening clips conducive to speaking. Students are expected to engage actively as they respond to the provided texts. The ultimate goal of this class is to enable students to fearlessly communicate their ideas/opinions in written/oral English.
