Syllabus_Korean
##
The purpose of this course is to re-examine and contemplate one of the 
strongest sense of political belonging, nation and its philosophical 
architect, nationalism. Readings will explore recent literature on the 
formation of nation and the development of nationalism, while being attentive 
to other forms of sub-national and supranational collective identities.
