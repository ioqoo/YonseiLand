Syllabus_Korean
##
The aim of this course is to explore the procedure for using basic visual 
elements and understanding visual languages. Dealing with the 
terms and theories of visual arts and design, students will practice various 
elements and organization of visual cultural fields.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course aims to present an overview of the design thinking involved at each 
stage of the design process.

Approaching inter-disciplinary contexts of visual culture, relating to art, 
design, technology, management and business administration, we will test out a 
number of thematic topics.
