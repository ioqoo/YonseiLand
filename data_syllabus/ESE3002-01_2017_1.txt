##
Fluid mechanics is an active field of research with broad applications to geophysics, astrophysics, meteorology, and biomedical sciences. 
This course studies the fundamental fluid mechanical notions, the mathematical analysis of simple fluid phenomena, and its practical engineering applications. 
Computational fluid dynamics (CFD) is also covered briefly.
