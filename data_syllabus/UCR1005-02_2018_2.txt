Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is one-credit using in-class online materials designed to improve reading and discussion skills at the Advanced, Honors, and native levels.  Students will gain confidence functioning in an academic English-speaking environment.  They will be exposed to authentic reading materials.  By participating in group discussions students will develop their academic English oral and aural fluency skills.
