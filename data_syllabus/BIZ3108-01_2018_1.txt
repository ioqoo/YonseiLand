Syllabus_Korean
##
관리회계의 목적은 기업내부의 정보이용자인 경영자에게 기업의 목표설정 및 달성에 도움을 줄 수 있는 유용한 회계정보를 제공하는 것이다.
따라서, 본 강의는 관리회계를 체계적으로 이해하는 것을 목표로, 유용한 회계정보를 기업 내부의 의사결정자에게 제공할 수 있는 방안에 대해 살펴보고자 한다.

Syllabus_English
##
* Course objectives: This course is intended to educate students into creative, ethical and global business leaders by introducing various topics in 
managerial accounting. This course will provide you with an understanding of the use of accounting information for managerial decision-making, planning, directing operational activities, and controlling. In this course, you will be introduced to basic managerial accounting concepts and techniques, and their applications in business decision-making such as cost behavior, cost estimation, budgeting, cost-volume-profit analysis and activity-based costing. In addition, Students need to consider ethical issues in managerial accounting.
