Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this class is to learn the basics of Judo. In this class, students will practice Judo skill, nagbeob, mechigi, and gudhigieul, according to the preference of each student. Therefore, every students learn about several kinds Judo skills. After the midterm exam, students concentrate on skills that chosen by each student. They will show their skills in the final exam.
