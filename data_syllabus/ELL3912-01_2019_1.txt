Syllabus_Korean
##
DISCLAIMER: This is a TENTATIVE online syllabus subject to change prior to the 
beginning of the course. A more detailed syllabus will be provided on the 
first day of class. 

This is an advanced speaking course designed to build on the skills learned in 
Speech and Debate. The focus of Speech and Communication is to further develop 
students’ communication skills that will enhance their ability to convey 
important information in English to specified audiences for a variety of 
purposes. Students will continue to work on the skills of organization and 
support, research, and effective delivery. Emphasis will be placed on 
enhancing presentation skills that will engage audiences and maintain their 
interest.
 
By the end of the semester, students will be able to:
- Prepare and deliver well-organized oral presentations in an 
interesting and professional manner that responds to the audience’s needs.
-Critique presentations in terms of its effectiveness in delivery, 
content, and/or use of visual aid.
