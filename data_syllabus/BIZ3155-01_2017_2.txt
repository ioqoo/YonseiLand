Syllabus_Korean
##
본 수업은 회계원리(1)과 중급회계(1)의 내용을 바탕으로 한국채택국제회계기준(K-IFRS)을 중심으로 세부적 회계주제에 대한 구체적인 회계처리방법, 재무제표 작성 및 공시방법을 학습하는 것을 목표로 한다. 
중급회계(2)는 중급회계(1)에서 다루지 않았던 금융부채, 자본, 주식기준보상과 기타 재무회계 주제인 주당이익, 리스회계, 법인세회계, 회계변경과 오류수정, 현금흐름표 등을 다룬다.

Syllabus_English
##
*Course objectives: Intermediate accounting course is designed to help 
students develop an in-depth understanding of financial accounting and 
reporting. The course is typically taken after completing Principles of 
Financial Accounting(1) BIZ1101 and Intermediate Accounting(1) BIZ3109. This 
course is the second in a series of two Intermediate accounting courses. The 
course covers in detail topics that are essential to reading, analyzing, and 
interpreting financial statements that are prepared in accordance with the 
Korean International Financial Reporting Standards(K-IFRS). This includes both 
procedural and conceptual issues, as well as consideration of matters related 
to presentation and disclosure.

* Course grading
The following items will be used to evaluate your performance in this course:
Midterm Exam            100 points
Final Exam              150 points
Attendance/Homework      50 points
Total                   300 points
Notes : Missing over a third of the class meetings will result in an automatic 
failing grade.
