Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will provide a broad overview of Korea, mainly focusing on its religion, society, culture, and history. Korea has one of the most diverse 
and dynamic religious cultures in the world. However, Korea witnessed very tubulent history in building a modern nation-state. 
Starting from the foundation of its nation which represents the Tan`gun (or Dangun) Myth, this course will survey how Korea has developed in East Asia and how it has been influenced by indigenous and foreign religions such as Sharmanism, Buddhism, Confucianism, Catholicism (or Western Learning), and Protestantism. This course will also examine Koreans` Independence Movements, New Women, Comfort Women, the Korean War, North Korea, and current Korean popular cultures which are dubbed as Hallyu or Korean Wave.
