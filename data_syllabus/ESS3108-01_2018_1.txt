Syllabus_English
##
This course focuses on the geological variables such as mineralogy and petrophysical properties such as porosity and permeability.
This course will apply the mineral diagenesis and its relation to the geotechnical properties.  In addition, biological factors may be applied to understand the geotechnical properties.
