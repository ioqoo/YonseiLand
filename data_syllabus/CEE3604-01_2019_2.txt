Syllabus_English
##
This course provides the industrial intership program. Students are expected to stay in a local construction field or laboratory to experience the on-going construction projects in which the theory and priniciples of civil and environmental engineering are practically applied. After completing the intership, the students` performance shall be evaluated by the field manager to determine `Pass` or `Non-pass`.
