Syllabus_Korean
##
Description:
The purpose of this course is to introduce Fencing to people who have never fenced before. Rules, technique, and strategy are taught 
so that by the end of the course students can fence and preside foil bouts. The course comprises footwork drills, attacks, parries, and sparring. 
Equipment is provided.
