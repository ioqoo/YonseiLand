Syllabus_English
##
History, theory and current trend of clinical psychology including assessment 
and treatment will be reviewed though didactic lectures, discussion, group 
presentation and assignments. 

Evaluation
1. Group Presentation 30%   
2. Mid & Final Exam 30% each 
3. Participation in psy experiments 5% (could substistute for other assignment 
upon permission)
4. Attendance 5%
