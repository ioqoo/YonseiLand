Syllabus_Korean
##
현대에 이르러 그 비중이 더욱 커진 생명과학의 내용을 파악하고 강의와 독서를 통해 인간의 삶과 환경에서 그 의미를 발견하는 것이 목표임. 이 교과목은 질병과 역사, 생물학의 탐구 방법, 진화, 성, 생명공학, 먹거리와 음식산업 등의 내용을 포함한다.

Syllabus_English
##
Nowadays, various fields of life sciences are prospering and we are facing biological issues almost everyday. Such life science days! Reflecting this trend, many books concerning biology are under enormous publicatuins. Therefore it is not hard to see biology thru reading various writters. Then, why don`t you try some books? The aim of this course is to give helps students in finding contents and meanings of life by use of biology-containing books. This course covers disease in history, study episodes, evolution, sex, bioengineering and an essay for foods.
