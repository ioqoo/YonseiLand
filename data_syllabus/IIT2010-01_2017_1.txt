Syllabus_Korean
##
This course consists of  two parts. The first part provides students with basic knowledge of 
philosophy of science and scientific methodology. Students will learn logic and methods of science 
mainly through historical case studies of Newton, Darwin and Einstein. From the perspective of Darwinian 
evolutionary theory, students will learn the conflict of interest between sexes, i.e., male-female
relationship. Einstein`s Special and General Relativity provide us scientific understanding of cosmology 
and time travel. The second part provides basic knowledge of history of technology, 
mainly recent development of IT, Bio tech, Automobile, and Service Industry
