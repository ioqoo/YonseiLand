Syllabus_Korean
##
The purpose of this class is to teach you how to think critically about arguments and discuss them in a clear and thoughtful manner through writing. Communication and critical thinking are important tools for us in our daily lives as citizens and are vital in any occupation. This class is designed to sharpen these skills. We will engage with various types of writing and media, from essays to poetry, movies to music. Through various genres and mediums, we will explore what it is to write an essay.
