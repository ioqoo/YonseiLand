Syllabus_Korean
##
To become a creative future business leader with global perspectives and integrity, in this course students will gain a practical understanding of how to manage a multinational company in the global marketplace. For this, the students will visit Korean MNCs doing business in Vietnam.
##
Syllabus_English
##
In this course, students in the global management track (GMT) will visit Korean and Chinese companies in Shanghai during the winter vacaton. After that, students will carry out a project on the entry strategy for the Chinese market. Students are required to present their project in the end of the semester.
