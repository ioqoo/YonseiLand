Syllabus_Korean
##
- 고전 시대 건반악기 음악 작품들과 대표적인 건반악기 문헌들에 대하여 공부한다.
- 고전 건반음악의 다양한 장르에 대해서 공부한다.
- 고전 시대적 특성과 음악양식에 따른 적절한 연주 해석법을 알아본다.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed for undergraduate piano music major students, in one semester, as a survey of the standard solo keyboard literature, covering repertoire from classic era to early romantic period.  The goal of this course is to help students acquiring a general knowledge of the most important keyboard repertoire; exploring the literature in terms of technique, interpretation, and performance; understanding the characteristic differences between periods and composers, and the stylistic practices that are most appropriate for each era, through intelligent listening to recordings or live performances perhaps; acquiring an appreciation for the rich heritage of keyboard masterpieces
