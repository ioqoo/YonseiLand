Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is intended to introduce fundamental concepts and framework for understanding multi-faceted aspects of organizations (i.e., structure, process, and behavior). It is designed as a series of contrasts among various theories classified by unit/level of analysis such as individuals, groups, and organizations. Applications cover a variety of Korean organizations mostly in the public sector. Ultimately, students will gain critical skills to analyze public organization(s) which will be especially helpful for those who plan to work in the (quasi-)public sector.
