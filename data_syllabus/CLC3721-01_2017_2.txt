Syllabus_Korean
##
A solid survey of Chinese literature and film in the modern and contemporary contexts (1919-2010).  Students are expected to comprehend the Chinese culture and society through the representations of writers and directors.  This class will follow a basically chronological outline while exploring differing regions of China: the May Fourth literature from 1919 to 1949, then mainland China during the Cultural Revolution and Taiwan in the middle of martial law.  After the 1980s, writing and films will be situated in the aftermath of the Cultural Revolution, the lift of the martial law in Taiwan, and the transitions made in Hong Kong.
