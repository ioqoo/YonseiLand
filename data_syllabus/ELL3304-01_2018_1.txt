Syllabus_English
##
This course aims to provide students with the opportunity to build a 
substantial knowledge of English grammar by reviewing practically all the 
grammatical aspects of the language and working on some, if deemed desirable, 
from the standpoint of descriptive grammar. The grading policy is as follows:
40 points for mid-term and final each, 10 points for assignments and/or presentations, 
5 points for class participation, and 5 points for attendance.
