Syllabus_Korean
##
Spanish 1 is designed for students who have not previously taken any Spanish or no prior knowledge of the language. The course emphasizes all four skills: speaking, listening, reading, and writing. Upon completion of this course, students will be able to use their newly acquired skills in order to communicate in Spanish in a realistic and culturally appropriate manner. All classes will be taught in English. The students will interact with their classmates in Spanish.
 Practice listening to, reading, speaking, and writing Spanish. 
Practice pronunciation skills.
Begin to develop their ability to communicate effectively and express themselves with confidence in Spanish. 

COURSE OBJECTIVES: Upon completion of this course, you will be able to: 
? Describe themselves and others 
? Compare and contrast people and ideas 
? Speak and write about current and future activities
? Express likes and dislikes
