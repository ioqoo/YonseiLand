Syllabus_Korean
##
To educate the fundamentals of semiconducor physics and device physics. Topics 
include Quantum physics for semiconductors, crystal structure, band theory, 
intrinsic and extrinsic semiconductors, p-n junction, FET, Bipolar transistor.
