Syllabus_Korean
##
This class reviews various tools of government introduced to solve challenging 
policy problems in a society. The tools of governments include direct 
government, government corporations, economic/social regulation, government 
insurance, public information, contracting, corrective tax, grants, loan 
programs, tax expenditure, vouchers, etc. Students are expected to improve 
their understanding of unique characteristics of each tool and apply 
conceptual understanding to practice of mixing tools for solving particular 
policy problems.
