Syllabus_Korean
##
The aim of this course is to learn various aspects of modern computer 
operating systems. Topic will include operating system structures, process 
management, memory management, storage management and I/O subsystems. A good 
background on computer architecture, algorithms and programming languages will 
be helpful. The principles of the Linux operating system will be covered as 
supplementary topics in the course.
