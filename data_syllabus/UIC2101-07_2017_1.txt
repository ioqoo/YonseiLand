Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is about reasoning, in theory and in practice. We will learn how different types of reasoning pertain to different topics, we will study some of the features of successful reasoning, and we will apply formal logic to the regimentation of philosophical argument. We will also practice reasoning in small group activities in order to become more creative and critical thinkers ourselves.
