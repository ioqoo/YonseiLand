Syllabus_Korean
##
이 과목은 HCI 기본 이론의 학습, 창의적 UX 디자인 방법론의 제시, 
프로젝트를 통한 아이디어의 구현, 평가 및 발표를 목표로 하며, 
programming, HCI, UX, 그리고 design에 배경지식이 없는 학생도 수강 가능하다.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course is for students without any knowledge about HCI and UX. Through 
lectures and a project, learn the fundamentals of human-computer interaction 
and creative design thinking. You work together for small problems, but long 
term project for yourself alone. Each week, in small design studios, present 
and discuss work with peers. The result applications are including software 
and hardware both. At the end of the course, students should present final 
works to the audiences.
