Syllabus_English
##
On completion of this course, students will be able to understand the 
followings:
1. Mathematical solution of differential equations related to the mechanical 
engineering 
2. Physical concept of the differential equations
3. Applications of the differential equations to mechanical engineering

The grades will be assigned based on the following weights:
one final exam : 40%
chapter exams: 50%
attendance: 10%
