Syllabus_English
##
This class will introduce the theoretical and historical origins of populism movement and the emergence of populist politicians, and investigate how and why populism movements recently surge across the world. Because this class opens at department of communication, the lecturer takes communication perspective investigating the populism phenomenon. Class participants MUST submit their own critics of course readings and prepare to interact with the lecturer as well as other classmates.
