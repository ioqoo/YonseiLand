Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a beginning badminton course in which students will develop the basic skills necessary to play the game of badminton. Technical skills include the overhead and underhand clears, the short & low serve, the high & deep serve, drop shots and the smash. Basic fitness and training principles will be discussed as applicable to the game of badminton.
