Syllabus_Korean
##
Christianity has contributed critically to the formation of the contemporary postmodern world. This class, to begin with, aims to explore the crux of Christianity, and assist students with gaining an enhanced understanding of it. Furthermore, students will be expected to learn about the interplay between Christianity and society today and obtain a deeper understanding and broader views of Christianity and the world.
