Syllabus_English
##
This Yonsei RC 102 course is designed to support the major and career 
exploration of 1st year YSB students. It will give them basic informations on 
academic disciplines about business administration and career fields with 
guest speakers and professors.
It will also encourage the students to participate in the class as 
an active learner, by making presentation on his(her) major decision and 
career planning.
