Syllabus_Korean
##
Students will be expected to be able to:
- do an individual presentation
- do a group/individual language project
- prepare for and give an individual oral exam
- master the vocabulary of the textbook, and any extra vocabulary related to 
the topics covered
- Prepare a paper of varying length (depending on topic) in journal style.  This does not need to be an actual research, but it can be a review of the literature, or even possibly an essay.
