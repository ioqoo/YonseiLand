Syllabus_Korean
##
본 수업은 시창 청음을 통하여 음악적 기술을 습득하는 것을 목표로 합니다. 시창청음 I, II를 통해 얻은 기본기를 바탕으로 시창청음 III은 3화음의 진행, 부7화음,차용화음등을 포함하는 단성, 2성, 화성진행 듣기로 확장됩니다. 연습 문제는 가능한한 실제 음악에서 발췌하는 방식으로 전반적인 음악적 이해를 돕게 할 예정입니다.

Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to improve musicianship skills through focusing on 
sight-singing and dictation. Based on the foundation obtained from Sight-
Singing and Dictation Class I and II, the ear training drills in this course 
will include enhanced 1-part melodic, 2-part, and harmonic progression 
dictations. Harmonic progressions will be extended from the basic triads to borrowed chords. 
Due to the nature of the course’s skill-based workshop approach, 
drills and quizes are essential for mastery of the material. As a result, 
students will be graded on attendance, quizes and tests. Excercises will be 
excerpted from well-known classical pieces, so that the course contents can 
actually help students for their overall understanding of music.
