Syllabus_Korean
##
The purpose of this course is to expand and refine the concepts and processes 
learned in undergraduate spatial analysis courses. You will be guided analysis 
techniques to different data types in urban phenomena, such as point, line, 
and polygons. The GIS Algorithm analysis abilities will be a major outcome from this 
course to identify urban spatial analysis.
