Syllabus_Korean
##
Understanding structure and function of human body in relation to movement 
based on basic anatomy for the application to better physical activity and 
exercise and injury prevention and rehabilitation
##
Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this course is for the student to develop an awareness of the 
functioning of the musculoskeletal system. The course begins by presenting the 
properties of the various biological materials (e.g. bones, ligaments, 
tendons, cartilage, and muscles). This is followed by analyses of the 
mechanics of the major joints of the body.

Laboratory work & quiz 30%, mid exam 30%, final exam 40%
