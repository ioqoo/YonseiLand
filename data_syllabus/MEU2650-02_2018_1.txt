Syllabus_English
##
Course Objectives:
The students will be able to:
1. understand the concept of dynamics.
2. apply the concept to various problems.
3. understand the examples of the applications to the real engineering 
problems.

Text & Recommended Readings
J. L. Meriam and L. G. Kraige, Engineering Mechanics, Dynamics (7th Edition), 
Wiley 
W. F. Riley and L. D. Sturges, Dynamics (2nd Edition), Wiley.
R. C. Hibbeler, Engineering Mechanics, Dynamics (11th Edition), Pearson 
Prentice Hall

Grading 
The grades will be assigned based on the following weights:
 Examinations : 80%, 
 Reports : 10%, 
 Diligence (measured by presence) and presentations : 10 %
