Syllabus_Korean
##
This class is aimed at exploring and developing integrated design skill through using new technological materials. Students will be encouraged to apply diverse design methods for framing issues and to create ideas through their own designs. 

Also focusing on main stages of design process from the idea to the end product. Starting from design concept, the class will complete with product marketing including packaging and advertisement. Students are expected to explore the purpose and the benefit of their own design. The task would require market research, problem solving and communication skills to be completed.
