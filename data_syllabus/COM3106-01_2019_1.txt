Syllabus_Korean
##
이 수업에서는 대인커뮤니케이션의 주요 개념과 이론들을 알아봅니다. 커뮤니케이션과 심리학 분야에 초점을 두고, 대인관계 형성 및 유지의 메카니즘을 이해함으로써, 우리의 (그리고 다른 사람들의) 대인커뮤니케이션 행동을 보다 잘 이해하고자 합니다.  관련 연구들을 살펴보면서 이러한 이론들이 현실세계에서의 커뮤니케이션 현상을 어떻게 설명하는지 알아볼 것입니다.

The goal of this course is to examine important concepts and theories in interpersonal communication and to understand how these principles and concepts can be used to promote effective interpersonal communication. Specifically, students will be introduced to theories in communication and psychology that help our understanding of the mechanisms underlying the development and maintenance of interpersonal relationships. Students will also be introduced to classical and recent research studies that apply these theories and concepts to explain our communication behavior in the real world contexts. At the end of the course, students will demonstrate their knowledge about communication concepts and theories required to evaluate their own communication relationships.
