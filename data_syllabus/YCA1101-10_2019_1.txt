Syllabus_Korean
##
Applying the historical and cultural perspectives, this course explores the encounter and expansion of Christianity in Northeast Asia. Despite sharing a Confucian culture, China, Japan, and Korea each reacted differently to the introduction and expansion of Christianity. Thus, this course examines how distinct historical and political contexts shaped the expansion of Christianity in Northeast Asia, with a particular focus on modern history.
