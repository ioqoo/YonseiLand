Syllabus_Korean
##
대기에서 일어나는 물리적 현상들에 대한 이해. 특히 건조 공기와 수증기의 열역학,

Syllabus_English
##
This course aims at understanding some basic and fundamental aspects of 
atmospheric physics. Mostly this course focuses on atmospheric thermodynamics 
and cloud physics. There will be two exams,mid-term and final. They cover 80% 
of the grade and the homework asssignments is responsible for the remaining 
20% of the grade.
