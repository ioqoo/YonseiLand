Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to give students an understanding of how a computer 
works its capabilities, limitations, and applications. This course has no 
prerequisite. Students will learn basic through intermediate computer concepts 
with an emphasis on the personal computer and its practical use, including 
hardware, application and system software, the Internet and World Wide Web and 
etc. The student is assigned lab exercises in word processor and spreadsheet 
and PowerPoint, preparing the individual to make productive use of these tools.
Grading By Assignment Weight: 
Attendance : 10% 
Homework : 20% 
Mid-term exam : 35% 
Final exam : 35%
