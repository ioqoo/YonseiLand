Syllabus_Korean
##
The course opens on multiple levels the global fashion business system. The mixture of topic lectures and group works, discussion sessions and 
presentation invites the students to experience fashion as a perfect preparation for further studies on that field and for the future profession.
Surprising knowledge will be shared, creativity and concepts, investigation and deeper reflextions are part of the learning goals.
The course will be teached in English only. There will be some assignment but the preparation time is in class only with the continuous support of the instructor.
