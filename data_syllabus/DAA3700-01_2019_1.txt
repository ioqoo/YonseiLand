Syllabus_Korean
##
Fundamentals of cell and molecular biology, which are required for chemical 
engineers for pursuing bio-related research/studies, will be discussed in this 
class. Furthermore, advanced cell and molecular therapeutics, including tissue 
engineering, gene therapy, stem cell therapy, and immune-therapy, will be 
introduced.
