Syllabus_Korean
##
Transport phenomena is often a core course in most universities. The basic 
theories discussed in the course deals with movement or what we now 
call "transport" of momentum, energy, and mass within the materials science 
application. Any reaction within materials processing will deal with some form 
of mass, energy, and/or momentum movement. The rate of this movement depending 
upon the driving force of the transport. Transport phenomena is more important 
today with ever-increasing importance in optimization of materials processing 
and synthesis. We will apply some partial differential equations and touch 
upon only the basic theories of the movement of momentum, mass, and heat.
