Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will teach diverse aspects of ICT and media industries such as policies, economics, technologies, business applications, the current and future trends of ICTs. In addition,  consumers` decision making process in the ICT and media industries will be discussed. For example, how a consumer makes a decision of buying a particular media or information product or service. After taking this class, students will better understand the aspects of ICTs & telecommunications mentioned above and will be able to find a proper career path in the ICT and media industries, if interested in the industries.
