Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed for students who have taken Korean (1) or have basic knowledge of Korean language. Emphasis is placed on the fundamentals of speaking, listening, writing, and reading. The objective of the course is to equip students with communicative skills. Students will engage in a variety of activities in speaking, listening, writing, and reading to improve their communication skills in Korean. In addition, students will become familiar with the language and cultural background of everyday life situations likely to be encountered in contemporary Korean society.
