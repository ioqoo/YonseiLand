Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will enable students to become familiar with both the reading and writing processes. Students will practice comprehending texts by analyzing and evaluating texts from various sources, including their peers. Also, understanding of the structure of a paragraph, essay, and rhetorical modes will enable students to apply their critical reading skills in organizing their ideas into coherent and cohesive writing. Through these practices, students will become effective writers and readers.
