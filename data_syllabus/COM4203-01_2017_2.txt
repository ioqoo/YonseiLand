Syllabus_Korean
##
This course has two objectives. 1) Students will learn about issues associated with the digital communication media, which includes lowering the quality of journalism, decline in media trustworthiness, group polarization or filter bubble effect and so on. 2) Students will be exposed to many cases of digital media startup, and have chances to initate and develop one`s own idea with a view to solving various issues of online communicaiton.
