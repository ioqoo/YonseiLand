Syllabus_Korean
##
1. To understand the wave characteristics of light waves (interference, diffraction, refraction, reflection) and to explore its applications.
2. To understand the basic principle behind optical imaging and apply it to develop a basic optical microscope.
3. To learn how to use the basic microwave simulation/design CAD tools as well as measurement tools.
4. To design, fabricate, and experimentally verify the performance of basic microwave passive circuits and components.
5. To understand the funtions of the high-frequency passive circuit components in practical microwave/optical communication systems.
