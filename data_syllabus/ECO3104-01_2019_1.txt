Syllabus_Korean
##
현실의 경제분석을 위해 경제이론과 현실의 데이터를 접목하는 각종 수단에 대하여 
공부. 경제이론에 부합되는 회귀모형의 추정 및 검정, 그리고 예측에 대한 논의가 주
된 내용임.

Syllabus_English
##
In economics, we need to cosult data for at least three purposes: to validate 
and to implement economic theories, to predict quantatatively the effects of a 
proposed policy, or simply to forecast the future. In this course, we will 
study basic theory and methods for such purposes. Topics include least 
squares, maximum likelihood, specification and misspecification tests, autocorrelation 
and heteroskedasticity corrections. Course evaluation will be based on one midterm(40%), one final(50%), 
assignments(5%), and class attendance(5%).
