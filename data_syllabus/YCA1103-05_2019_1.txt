Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this course is to help students understand the development of 
Christianity, and the meaning of the Bible and core message of the Bible 
regarding ecology in our Globalised World. We will try to examine the biblical 
spirit critically, in order to promote eco justice, with a view to application 
to our daily life.
Process of the class
-To learn his/herstory of Christianity
-To explore alternative understanding in the Bible in terms of Ecology
-To promote Eco awareness
-To develop alternative solution & leadership in our own context
