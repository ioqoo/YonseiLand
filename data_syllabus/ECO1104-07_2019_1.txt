Syllabus_Korean
##
The aim of this course is to introduce students to macroeconomics. To this end, this course covers various topics in macroeconomics at an introductory level. Topics include the economic growth, long-run equilibrium of the economy, monetary system, short-run fluctuations, and macroeconomic policy.
