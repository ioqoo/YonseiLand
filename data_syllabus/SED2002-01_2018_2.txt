Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is the laboratory companion course to the lecture-based Organic Chemistry course. However, it is a completely separate course in terms of grade and credit. The laboratory course is an introduction to the major concepts of chemical investigation, organic chemistry, spectroscopy, structure determination and most common laboratory techniques. One of the objectives of this course is to familiarize students with standard laboratory settings. This includes using chemical reagents and chemical equipment, keeping professional laboratory notes and use safe laboratory techniques and procedures.
