상화해내는 일입니다. 그러기 위해서 무엇보다도 중요한 덕목은 다양한 문화현상에

할 수 있을 것이며, 궁극적으로는 고유한 목소리로 대중문화현상에 대한 자신의개념

Syllabus_English
##
Cultural studies is a completely new comer, which came into the spotlight 
on the academic stage within two decades. Paradigm shift in the field of 
scholarly inquiry caused by the dynamics of contemporary social and cultural 
trends created it in an unprecedented way. Emerging out of nowhere cultural 
studies embarks on a new career as a precursor of modern academic genre. 
Actually culture is a concept that has remained on the top of the agenda 
within the human and social sciences for two decades. It incites various 
controversy and debate, drawing sometimes dubious and at times strongly 
supportive attention. Under the academic circumstances, cultural studies will 
be soon the staple of academic fields with interconnecting and networking 
power of every science. This very potential is going to make cultural studies 
to lay the foundation for students build up their own perspective of social 
and cultural phenomena.  
   To understand the concept of culture and cultural studies, in this class we 
need to read lots of various texts and discuss of them. From which level? 
Certainly we are going to start at the beginner level dealing with the very 
fundamental principles. In doing so, this class will get you exposed to 
sophisticated theoretical texts in a very gradual and clear way. Of course, it 
still will take considerable effort for you to get there, because there is no 
such meaningful thing which can be so fast built up without reasonable effort 
and hard work. If you are fully committed to it with compelling desire, 
however, your exertion ultimately will bring you to a higher intellectual 
level as undergraduate students. And going through this class, you are able to 
get prepared for the true test as a challenging intellectual with promising 
potential.
