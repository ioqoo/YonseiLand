Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to improve the awareness of the current event/ issues and the general English skills of students. Students will discuss, read, present and write about current event topics around them through various media form of reporting of them. Through active listening, speaking and reading, writing practices, students will become critical listeners, persuasive speakers and effective writers.
