Syllabus_Korean
##
By the end of the course students should:
1. Have improved ability to use English in particular science and 
engineering subject areas.
2. Have a wider view of different topics in modern science and 
engineering.
3. Have improved English writing skills.
4. Have improved English speaking skills.
5. Be able to give opinions in English which go beyond simple factual 
expressions.
6. Be more confident in English communication.
7. Be more aware of their individual English strengths and weaknesses.
