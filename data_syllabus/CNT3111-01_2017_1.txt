Syllabus_Korean
##
Objectives:
1. To learn thermal, sensorial and body movement comfort aspects of clothing
2. To study requirements and types of eco-friendly textiles according to the trend of eco-friendly clothing
3. To understand the function of body protective clothing
4. To acquire principles and functions of smart clothing and textiles

Outline:
1. Lecture: To learn four(4) themes such as comfort, eco-friendliness, safety and convergence technology aspects of clothing
2. CBL(Community Based Learning): To do CBL by participating some clothing an textile institutes related to the lecture themes
##
