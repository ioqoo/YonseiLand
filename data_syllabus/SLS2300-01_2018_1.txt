The class is for students who want to improve their soccer skills from basics to advanced to play well in soccer game. 
If you take the course, you will be able to play an essential role in soccer games with skills as well as teamwork.

** The final grade will be assessed on four main domains:
  30% for class attitude, 20% for final-term, 20% for league scores, 30% for attendance
