Syllabus_English
##
This course is aimed at students who are coming to immunology for the first 
time. Cell Biology is recommended as a prerequsite course. In this course, we 
will discuss the cells and molecules of the immune system and how they work 
together in providing defenses against invading foreigns. We will also focus 
on consequences that arise from inadequacies of the immune systems. Two exams 
(45% each) will be scheduled soon.
