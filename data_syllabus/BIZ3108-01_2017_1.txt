Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is to provide an introduction to management accounting with emphasis on the management use of accounting information for planning and 
control, product costing, budgeting and decision making purposes.

The course intends to educate students into creative, ethical, and global business leaders by introducing basic managerial accounting concepts and 
techniques, and their application in creative and global business decision-making.
