Syllabus_Korean
##
기초적인 러시아어를 배운다.
- 러시아어의 발음과 문자를 익힌다.
- 러시아어 초급 문법을 학습한다. (교재 약 12과까지 커버함: 이후 나머지 내용은 같은 교재를 사용하여 러시아어(2)로 연계)
- 러시아어 회화의 간단한 표현들을 학습한다.
- 러시아에 간다면 짧은 기간 관광객으로서 살아남을 수 있을 만한 수준의 러시아어를 학습하는 것이 러시아어(1)의 궁극적인 목표이다.

Syllabus_English
##
Russian (1) is for students who have never learned Russian and therefore do not know any Russian at all. We begin by learning basic Russian pronunciation and memorizing the Russian alphabet. (you have to memorize, no way around it) Then we proceed to learn basic Russian grammar and everyday expressions such as "hello," "please," and "thank you." The goal of this course is to make students learn enough Russian to survive in Russia for a short period of time as tourists. This course is taught in Korean but the instructor can provide explanations in other languages if needed. The course material is also in Korean. Russian (1) will cover up to Lesson 12 of the book and then the rest will be covered in Russian (2).
