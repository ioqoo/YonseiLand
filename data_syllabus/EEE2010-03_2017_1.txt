Syllabus_Korean
##
The objective of this course is to develop an ability to understand the basic electric circuit theory such as techniques of circuit analysis, OP-Amp, RLC circuits, 3-phase circuit, Laplace transform, etc. Also, the students will carry out the computer simulation exercises with PSPICE software.
