Syllabus_Korean
##
This course deals with design innovation from different perspectives; business, design and culture.

This course includes series of lectures and seminars on design innovations and its process.

Students will develope their own strategy of design innovation by developing their own projects (project #1), investigating its process and impact on user group`s life style.

Students are expected to learn design innovation in broader terms in relation with social structure.
