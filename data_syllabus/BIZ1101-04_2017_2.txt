Syllabus_Korean
##
After taking this course, students will be able to apply fundamental knowledge of
accounting to the financial statement interpretation. They will also understand the 
basic corporate valuation framework and be aware of ethical issues related to 
the public disclosure of financial statement information.
##
Syllabus_English
##
[Repeat] This course is intended as an introduction to accounting theory and 
practice and its function as the communication medium of the modern business 
information system.  The objective of this course is provide prospective 
managers an awareness and understanding of financial accounting fundamentals.
Grades are based on exams 70%, participation 20%, and attendance 10%.
