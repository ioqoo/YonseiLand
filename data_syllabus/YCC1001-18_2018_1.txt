Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic College English I is an integrated, two-credit course designed to 
develop fundamental English skills for students at the basic level. This course 
will enable students to effectively use basic strategies and four skills to 
communicate in English.  Students will be able to clearly identify parts of a 
sentence and recognize meaningful units through reading and listening 
practices. with extensive exercises, students will also develop their 
writing and speaking skills at the sentence level. Through this cours students 
are expected to develop their English proficiency.
