Syllabus_Korean
##
To understand the mathematical concept of probability phenomenon in nature or 
science and discuss the meaning and the basic properties of the mathematical 
model of probability phenomenon. 
In addition, summarize and deal with the data from experiment or observation 
through probability phenomenon and based on that, deal with the basic concepts 
of the statistical inference to obtain mathematical properties.  And introduce
 effective methods for estimation and statistical testing.
Students will be practicing data analysis using an appropriate program language
(ex. R) at a computer lab.
