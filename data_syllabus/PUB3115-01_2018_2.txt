Course Description
This course focuses on public policy case study. This course is organized by two section. First, the instructor will talk about and advise how  to make a public policy case study. Second, all students will conduct their own case studies. What is the most import is that this course is community based learning (CBL). It means that students should choose policies at the community level such as government offices in Seoul (e.g., Seodaemun-gu office), public museum, public library, community center, etc. All students are highly recommended to conduct a research in the field. 

Goals
1. How to conduct a case study
2. Experience of a case study 
3. Understanding public policies at the community level
