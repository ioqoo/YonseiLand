Syllabus_English
##
The class covers as follow;
1) Part I General Provisions and Part II Commercial Activities of Commercial Act of Korea
2) Introduction of Bills of Exchange and Promissory Notes Act of Korea

It aims to understand basic concepts and principles of the Acts.

A ratio of grading is as follows;
80% Exam (Mid-term 40%, Final 40%)
20% Attendence, Participation, etc.
