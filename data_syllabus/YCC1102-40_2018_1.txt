Syllabus_Korean
##
The overall aim of this course is to help students develop a positive attitude and mindset towards English, so that they will no longer have fears or stress about upgrading their English language skills. 

The goals of this course are to help students:

- be more confident and effective when writing academic essays and reports in English;
- be more confident and effective when reading difficult academic texts in English;
- improve their atttude and enjoyment of developing their English language skills by their own efforts.
