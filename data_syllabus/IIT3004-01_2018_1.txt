Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will cover from basics of digital circuit design using verilog HDL (Hardware Discription Language)
to advanced topics of digital systems such as processor organization and 
system on chip design. We assume that the attendees have programing skill of C 
language. Based on the digital system design, investigating new area of 
digital system for application of ICT convergence.
##
