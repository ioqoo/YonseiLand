Syllabus_Korean
##
In this course you will be given the opportunity to build on your existing knowledge of English by improving your core skills: Reading, speaking, listening, and writing. The course is based on the textbook and materials approved by the Univeristy.
Course Objectives: The activities in this course have been designed to help you communicate in English more fluently and accurately in a natural , confident manner.  Each lesson had been designed to improve all 4 skills: reading, listening, speaking and writing. Naturally, students will be provided with ample opportunities to improve their speaking skills while in class by sharing answers, ideas and opinions with classmates. To achieve this I would like you to speak in English at all times in class.
