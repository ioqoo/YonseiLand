Students will learn general theories and techniques on policy-making process and evaluation. Also the class introduces real-life examples on public policy and students will learn an analytical framework to understand the daily operation of government and publc sector.

This class is designated as a Social Innovation Certificate (SIC) course. The course contents and practices will be related to social innovation and solving social problems.
