Syllabus_Korean
##
How do people identify and interpret sounds, words, sentences, and larger discourses? How do speakers produce language? What are the mechanisms that children go through when learning language? Psycholinguistics is an interdisciplinary field that explores the cognitive processes underlying language use and acquisition. In this course, you will learn about the central issues, key findings, and experimental techniques of psycholinguistics.
