Syllabus_Korean
##
인지과학의 핵심인 `마음`의 개념을 이해하고, 인지과학과 관련된 여러 영역들(심리학, 컴퓨터과학, 신경과학, 언어학, 철학)과 연구주제(지식표상, 의식, 언어 발달, 기억, 주의, 시각, 등)를 최근 논문과 발췌된 교과서를 중심으로 탐색한다.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course will introduce the various topics in Cognitive Science. The major 
topics to be included will be Knowledge Representation, Consciousness, 
Language Acquisition, Memory, Attention, Visual Perception, and etc. The 
course will consist of lectures and discussions on recent research 
articles and chapters from the above areas. There will be student 
presentations by a small group and mid & final-term exam (exams could be 
replaced by group projects depending on the number of students).
