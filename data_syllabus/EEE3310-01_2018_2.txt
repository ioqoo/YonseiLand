Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will introduce how to design control systems for a given physical 
system. It will cover mathematical models of physical systems and various
feedback characteristics. Control examples will be illustrated to help 
understandings of control theory.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course covers mathematical models of physical system, feedback 
characteristics of control systems, time response analysis, control design 
specifications, stability, root locus technique, frequency response analysis, 
and state variable analysis and design.
