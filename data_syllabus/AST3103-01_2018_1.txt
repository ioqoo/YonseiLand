Syllabus_Korean
##
As the first of two-semester courses, this course delivers basic concepts and principles of Astrodynamics, and thus provides foundations for studying Astrodynamics 2 and relevant senior/graduate courses.

Specific topics cover two-body problem, Kepler`s equation and Kepler`s problem, coordinate and time systems, observations and sensor systems, celestial phenomena, etc.
