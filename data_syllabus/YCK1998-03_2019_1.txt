고

(IT)의 
제3의 물결이며 유비쿼터스 정보사회의 도래에 따라 우리의 생활 스타일이 변화되고

(IT)의 
발전에 대해 고찰한다. 마지막으로 유비쿼터스 사회가 당면하고 있는 과제로서 IT 자

Syllabus_English
##
We study the characteristics of Ubiquitous that can realize the ubiquitous 
society. We also study various services that the ubiquitous society provides as 
well as the worldwide ubiquitous related projects and industries. We try to 
understand ubiquitous computing, network and other key technologies that can 
implement the ubiquitous society. We also study the evolution of the ubiquitous 
information technologies(IT). We review the outstanding issues of the 
ubiquitous 
society including shortages of IT resources, information security and privacy, 
etc. 
Finally, we discuss the Post ubiquitous to come such as IoT(Internet of thing) 
and
4th industrial revolution

  Home work 10%
  Quiz 20%
  Mid-term Exam 30%
  Final Exam 30%
