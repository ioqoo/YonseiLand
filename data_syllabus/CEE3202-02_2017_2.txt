Syllabus_Korean
##
Surveying is the fundamental of civil and environmental engineering.  This 
course will develop students` knowledge of (1) geo-spatial measurements of 
common civil and environmental engineering projects; (2) the types of 
instruments, techniques and detailed procedures used for making geospatial 
measurements in a field environment; (3) the techniques involved in 
collecting, processing and plotting the types of geospatial data necessary for 
constructing engineering-scale maps; (4) Understanding of spatial reference 
frameworks; (5) effective approaches to setting up and presenting solutions of 
positioning/location problems.
