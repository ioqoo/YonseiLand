Syllabus_Korean
##
이 수업은 교육학 전공생들에게 교육심리학 전반에 걸친 주요 이론을 소개하고, 교육 개선을 위한 적용 능력을 향상시키는 것을 주요 목적으로 한다. 교육의 심리적 기제, 학습자와 교수자, 교수-학습과정 등에 대한 이해를 넓히고, 교육적 함의를 논의한다.

Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed for students majoring in Education to provide an overview of Educational Psychology. The course will help students develop an understanding of psychological ideas and research relevant to education and conceptualize their application to educational practice. 

*Participation: 10% 
*Midterm: 30 %  
*Final: 30 %            
*Group Presentation: 30 %

*Fail policy: You will get an F 1) if you get a 0 on any of them above, or 2) if you are absent more than 3 times. 
*Bonus: There will be a few opportunities to get an extra bonus (e.g., extra assignments, experiment participation, attending to colloquium talks, etc.)
