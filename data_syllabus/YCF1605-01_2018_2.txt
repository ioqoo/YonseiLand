Syllabus_English

*Syllabus in English

This subject aims at acquisition of the practical Arabic which widely is 
spoken in the Arab countries(22 countries) and Islamic countries(57 countries) by using the basic 
expressions. Especially it introduces the essencials of Arabic grammar and the 
daily expressions of the Arabs.
