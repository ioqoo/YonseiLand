Syllabus_English
##
This course is industrial intership program. The students will stay in a local 
field or laboratory to experience the real projects and construction in which 
the theory and priciples of civil and environmental engineering are applied 
and practiced. After the field experience, the students` performance will be 
determined based upon the field manager`s evaluation (Pass vs. Non-Pass 
evaluation). During the field internship, the department professor(s) will 
visit the sites where the students are having internship experience.
