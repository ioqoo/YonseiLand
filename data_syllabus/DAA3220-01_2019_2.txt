Syllabus_English
##
The object of this class is to understand basic ideas of chemical reaction 
kinetics starting from motion of molecules to the application of reaction rate 
laws to analyze the kietics of various chemical reactions.
The total grade is scored as follows: mid exam 33%, final-exam 33%, and 
assignments (and attendance) 33%.
