Syllabus_Korean
##
The course will provide an introduction to Greek tragedy and the major theories of the tragic. In particular, we will focus on Aeschylus’ Oresteia, Sophocles’ Oedipus the King, Euripides’ Medea, and nineteenth- and twentieth-century theories of the tragic (from German Idealism and Arthur Schopenhauer to Søren Kierkegaard, Georg Simmel, Walter Benjamin, and Peter Szondi).
