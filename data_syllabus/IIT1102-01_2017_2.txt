Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed for undergraduates pursuing the individual research 
study. The student is supposed to work with professors of her/his research 
interests and decides the individual research topic with the supervisory 
professor. After the completion of the individual research study, the student 
shall submit their final report with some outcomes to the supervisory 
professor. The outcome might include a report, a presentation, an apparatus or 
a software package, and will be graded by the supervisory professor.
