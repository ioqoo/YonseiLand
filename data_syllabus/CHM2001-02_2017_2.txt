Syllabus_English
##
Students Requirement: Second grade students and higher major in wind 
instrument, 1-2 people per part
Course Description& Goal: Students acquire wide expert knowledge by discussing 
international musical trends or basic theory based on foreign journals
Course Method: Each part investigate, discuss and present journal
Grade Evaluation: Attendance and class participation
