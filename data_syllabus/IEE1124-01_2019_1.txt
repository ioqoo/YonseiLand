Syllabus_Korean
##
This course gives general understanding of Korean Culture. The course will provide useful information concerning both Traditional Korean culture and Modern Korean culture. By the end of the course students will be able to deepen understanding on basic concepts and ideas of Korean culture.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course gives general understanding of Korean culture. It is designed for foreign students to have knowledge in traditional and modern Korean culture. 
The course consists of reading assignments. Class discussions, presentations are integral parts of the course requirements.
