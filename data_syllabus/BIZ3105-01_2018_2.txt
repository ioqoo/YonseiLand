Syllabus_Korean
##
회사제도는 오늘날 자본주의 발전에 핵심적인 역할을 하고 있다. 이 강의는 회사의 설립, 운영, 해산 등 회사와 관련된 주요한 법률 문제를 다룬다. 회사법의 주요 현안과 법적 쟁점에 대한 논의를 통해서, 회사관련 법률 문제 및 분쟁에 대한 해결능력을 제고한다.

Syllabus_English
##
This course will familiarize you with the organization of modern business firms and the rules designed to minimize conflicting interests among firm constituents. This course covers: the structure and characteristics of modern business associations especially the creation and organization of corporations; the distribution of corporate power between management and shareholders with emphasis on fiduciary duties of directors, officers and controlling shareholders. In addition to traditional analysis, this course also attempts to develop skills in statutory construction through regular application of Korean commercial laws and in giving clients prospective advice as to planning and structuring corporate transactions.
