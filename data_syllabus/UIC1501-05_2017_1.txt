Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is a general introduction to European history from Ancient Greece to the beginning of World War I. Topics covered in the course include Ancient Greece and Rome, the Middle Ages, the Renaissance and Reformation and Enlightenment, the Political and Industrial Revolutions, Imperialism and the outbreak of the First World War. As a relatively basic introduction to European history, the course is geared towards students who are studying this particular aspect of world history for the first time.
