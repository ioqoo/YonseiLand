Syllabus_Korean
##
현대 회사제도의 핵심인 한국 회사법제에 대한 내용을 이해하고 최근 해당 분야의 핵
심 논제에 대해 학습함으로써 기업지배구조에 대한 이해도를 제고함.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This class will try to investigate the detailed contents of the corporations 
part under the Korean Commercial Code(KCC), and provide students with the 
ability to further research on this area. Furthermore, this corporations class 
will focus on current debates on corporate law in other developed countries in 
order to improve students` understanding on global corporate issues.
