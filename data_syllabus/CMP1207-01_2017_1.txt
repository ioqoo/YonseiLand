Syllabus_English
##
This course attempts to promote students` ability to establish a rough picture 
of electroacoustic music as well as to acquaint them with music notation 
software as a part of the musical activities using computer. Every student 
will be expected to complete two assignments, a midterm project and a final 
project, in lieu of written exams.
