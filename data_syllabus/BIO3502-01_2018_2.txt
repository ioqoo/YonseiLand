Syllabus_Korean
##
1) This course is to understand the structure of proteins. Students 
overexpress a gene in bacteria, purify the protein, crystallize it, get the X-
ray diffraction data, and finally determine the protein structure
2) The goal of the second half of the course is to have hands-on experience on 
genomics, especially microbial genomics through genomic DNA isolation, genome 
sequencing, assembly, annotation, and analysis.
