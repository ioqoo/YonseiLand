Syllabus_Korean
##
Light-matter interaction has a wide variety of applications such as display, 
communication, device fabrication by lithography etc. This course is intended 
to help materials science and engineering majors understand the basic concepts 
of optics and optoelectronics, which covers Maxwell`s equation, law of 
reflection and refraction, interference and diffraction, and light propagation 
in media.
