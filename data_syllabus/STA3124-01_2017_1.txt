Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed for students who want to learn how probability theory 
can be applied to the study of real-world phenomena. This course will cover 
the basics of discrete and continuous time Markov chain theory, and other 
stochastic processes. Topics include the random walk, Poisson processes, birth and death processes, and Brownian motion.
