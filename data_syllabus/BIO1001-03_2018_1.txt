##
This course provides a foundational understanding of the fundamental principles of life science, focusing on understanding basic life processes at the cellular and molecular level.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course focuses on building a general understanding of life science. The class covers basic chemistry necessary to understand biology, the chemical and biological context of life, the structure and function of cells and membranes, the energy metabolism in animals and plants, and basic concepts of genetics. Laboratory courses will give students the opportunity to learn and experience lab methodologies while providing background knowledge for the class.
