Syllabus_Korean
##
This class aims to help students improve their ability to communicate effectively in business-related environments.  Students will get extensive practice and feedback composing various types of written business texts, such as emails, letters, and reports. In addition, they will also have the opportunity to participate in group discussions and to deliver presentations on various issues relevant in entrepreneurial and corporate settings.
