Syllabus_Korean
##
To learn scientific computing and to be familiar with programming language:
Scientific computing is concerned with the design and analysis of algorithms 
for solving mathematical problems that arise in computational science and 
engineering. In this course we can learn programming to concentrate on 
problems in scientific computation. In particular, we will focus on numerical 
linear algebra such as solving linear systems in various applications.

Programming Languages: C, Fortran
Mathematical Software System: Matlab
