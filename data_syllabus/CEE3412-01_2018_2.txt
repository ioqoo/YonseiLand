Syllabus_Korean
##
Hydrology can be defined as the science that deals with space-time 
characteristics of the quantity and quality of the waters of the earth,  
encompassing their occurrence, movement, distribution, circulation, storage, 
exploration, development, and management. The objective of this course is to 
educate Civil Engineers who can design hydraulic structures by acquiring the 
basic principles in Hydrology.
