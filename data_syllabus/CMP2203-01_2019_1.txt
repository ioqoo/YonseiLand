Syllabus_English
##
.
In this course, we will deepen our understanding of tonal harmony languages
extending it to include dominant seventh chord, modulation, secondary dominant chords and secondary diminished chords. 
Through the course, students can enable to analyze a number of musical masterworks and will be able to perform or conduct a work from an informed point of view with your own interpretation and clear musical evidence.
