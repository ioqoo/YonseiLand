Syllabus_Korean
##
Clothing and the sensibility is from the sensation of color, texture, olf
olfactory, auditory and tactile. This course helps students understand 
perceptional mechanism of clothing sensibility satisfying human`s emotion. The 
course also discusses statistical approaches and the case studies related with 
clothing sensibility.

Syllabus_English
##
This course introduces the definition of clothing sensibility, human 
sense/sensibility, its data process and analysis to evaluate users` sensibility 
and satisfaction. Also, the course designs research related with humans
sensibility.
