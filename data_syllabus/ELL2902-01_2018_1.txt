Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a tentative on-line syllabus and is subject to change. 

As an introductory class to the field of interpreting and translation, this course offers an overview of the field`s basic principles. 

After completing this course, students will understand the basic elements that comprise interpreting/translation, be able to apply the techniques introduced in class to improve their interpreting/translation skills, and understand the criteria against which interpreting performances and translations are evaluated.
##
Syllabus_English
##
As an introductory class to the field of interpreting, this course offers an overview of the basic elements that comprise interpreting/translation. 

Students will be introduced to the activities and techniques used to improve interpreting skills, such as source text analysis, paraphrasing, sight 
translation, and note-taking/symbolization.
