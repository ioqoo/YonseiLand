Syllabus_Korean
##
Through performing process designs, the students will learn the design procedure of reaction and separation units and how to use design software. Team project can make students find how to get teamwork, leadership, presentation skill. In addition, this class will teach the fundamental of artificial intelligence to develop the students` capability solving chemical problems in broader aspects.
