Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to introduce the fundamental skills, rules and strategies of Kumdo. The continuous stroke improves cardio respiratory 
endurance, muscular endurance, and agility. During combination strokes, the students will learn essential movement for sparring. This course will enable students to enjoy kumdo as a recreational lifetime activity.
