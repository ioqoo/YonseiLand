Syllabus_English
##
The main purpose of this course is to examine the major themes in neo-
institutional theories of formal organizations. This course is designed to 
explore how institutionalist research applied to organizational behavior has 
emerged and evolved over time. We will review the foundations, current status, 
and emerging new directions of the key theoretical approaches of 
organizational institutionalism.
