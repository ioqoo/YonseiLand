Syllabus_English
##
This course deals with the rational choice of consumers and firms and the stucture and effects of markets where they play. 
The consumer theory is concerned with the explanation of how a consumer determine his/her demnad for commodities.
The firm theory takes care of the firm`s behavior and its supply of good to the market.
The market theory tells us about the resource allocation and general equilibrium.
We also deal with the market failure and it effects on economic welfare.
