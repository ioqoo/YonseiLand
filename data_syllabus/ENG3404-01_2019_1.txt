Syllabus_Korean
##
- Start up준비를 위한 다학제적 협력 
- 창의적 문제해결 능력 개발
- 프로젝트 관리 능력 개발
- 팀웍
- 사회적 책무성 개발
- 공학적 해결책에 대한 발표 및 시제품 만들기

본 과목은 고등교육혁신원 사회혁신가 인증 교과목에 해당하며, 수업과정 중 사회혁신 및 시회문제 해결과 관련한 내용의 강의 및 실습이 함께 포함됩니다
아울러 수업 내 학생 제출 결과물 중 일부는 고등교육혁신원에서 추후 사회혁신 활동 결과물로 활용될 수 있습니다.

- Multidisciplinary cooperation to prepare for start up
- Development of creative problem solving ability
- Development of  Project management ability 
- Development of Teamwork
- Development of social accountability
- Presentation of engineering solutions and prototyping

This course is a certification course for social innovators of the Higher Education Innovation Institute.
It focuses on hands-on practices for social innovation and problem solving
Some of the results of student submissions in the class can be used as results of future social innovation activities at the Higher Education Innovation Institute.

Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to foster students` project management and start up by
interdisciplinary collaborative experience. Students will link 
social economic environmental, legal, political consideration with 
technological design and innovation. Students in this class will get the 
leadership, teamwork, communication skill, creative problem solving ability  
as well as entrepreneurship. They will have the characteristics necessary to 
meet the new challenges in changing world.
