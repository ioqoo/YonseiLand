Syllabus_English
##
This course provides an intense overview and review of various elements of contemporary English usage.  The two primary goals of this course are
(1) to explore and learn a wide range of usage problems associated with contemporary grammar in both speech and writing; and
(2) to understand the rationale behind the use of English and the importance of authentic linguistic data.

Each class will proceed with the following:
Quiz, lectures, questions & answers, and discussions.

No Prerequisite

Grading
1. Attendance and participation (10%)
2. Pop quizzes (10%)
3. Two Miderm exams (30%)
4. Final exam (35%)
5. Presensentation (15%)
