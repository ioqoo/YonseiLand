Syllabus_Korean
##
In today’s world, the debate on immigration has a strong impact on the direction of political decisions, including the recent Brexit, Syrian refugee crisis, terrorism in Europe, and the upcoming US President’s election. At the same time, many scholars have paid attention to the development potential of international migration for the benefit of both societies and migrants and to contribute to sustainable development and poverty reduction. This course is intended to understand the various aspects of transnational migration such as national policies, economic benefits, social costs, and potential for development. By doing so, the class will comprehend the diverse geographical issues from a marginal perspective of migrating population.
