Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will be an introductory course on modern world issues 
(contemporary social ethics).  Christian ethics, as well as the philosophical 
views of John Stuart Mill and Immanuel Kant will be covered.  Throughout the 
term, we will examine themes and issues on contemporary moral philosophies, 
with particular emphasis on applied ethics.  Applied ethics involves examining 
specific controversial issues (please refer to weekly topics for further 
information)

Few questions to consider:  "How does one go about behaving ethically?",  "How 
do we define ethical behavior?"
What is the moral/ ethical duty of a global leader?
