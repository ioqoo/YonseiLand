Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is a survey of British literature from the Romantic to the Modernist period. We will study major authors and their works along with pertinent social, cultural, political, and philosophical movements during this period. The primary goal of this course is for students to gain a comprehensive knowledge about British literature from Romanticism through Modernism. In addition, we aim to learn to analyze various literary genres and critically engage with literary concepts.
