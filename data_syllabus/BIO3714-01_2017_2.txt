Syllabus_Korean
##
This course focuses on how the two main classes of contaminants, inorganic and 
organic, are harmful to ecosystems at each level of biological hierarchy: 
molecular, cellular, tissue, organ, individual, population, community, 
ecosystem, and global.  Major concepts include bioaccumulation, trophic 
transfer, biomagnification, biotransformation, detoxification, elimination, 
bioavailability, and toxicant effects.
