Syllabus_Korean
##
Students will discuss, read, present and write about current events/ research topics of their interest. 

The course aims to develop skills to analyze, organize and present research in forms of oral communication: debate, discussion and presentation.  Enhancement of the critical thinking skill will be the focus of the course and students will revisit necessary academic and professional communication skills to perform well in the coursework.
