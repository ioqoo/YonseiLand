Syllabus_Korean
##
This course introduces students to the principles of foodservice systems. It discusses the basic principles of foodservice management and operations, including foodservice systems, organizational structure, leadership, human resources, financial management, and marketing. This course discusses the fundamental theories and practices as to qualifications, knowledge, techniques, and personality to become competent managers for non-commercial foodservice operations.
