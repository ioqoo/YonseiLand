Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will empower students to reinforce and acquire English language skills using science and technology-based themes as content.  Students will engage in group projects, discussion, debate, and presentations; they will be encouraged to analyze and educate peers on topics derived directly from other courses related to their majors, as well as topics of personal interest.
