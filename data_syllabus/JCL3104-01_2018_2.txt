##

##
Syllabus_English
##
Focusing on transaction between private parties, the central goal of this class to understand the importance of international business transaction and the legal framework surrounding the topic. Mainly, we will deal with the following issues: (1) What are the subjects of international business transactions?; (2) How do we make such international business transaction? (3) How do we resolve disputes arising out of such international business transactions?
