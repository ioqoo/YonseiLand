Syllabus_Korean
##
This course introduces brand theory and fashion cases and also focuses on the 
development of new fashion brands by the brand project. Topics include brand 
analysis, trends in branding, fashion branding strategy, and future fashion 
branding.

Syllabus_English
##
-This course introduces brand theory and fashion cases and also focuses on the 
development of new fashion brands by the brand project. Topics include brand 
analysis, trends in branding, fashion branding strategy, and future fashion 
branding.
-The format comp rises a blend of seminar and lecture modes.
Lecture and Case Analysis 2 Hours
Discussion & Brand Group Project 2 Hours
-Evaluation
Class Attendance and Participation(20%)
Midterm Exam(30%) 
Group Project(50%)
