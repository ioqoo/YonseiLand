Syllabus_Korean
##
외국인 대상 중국어어법의 교육이론과 실제 상황에서 쓰이는 현상 등을 학습하고 분석하여 실용적인 제2외국어로서의 중국어 교육어법을 익히는 데 목적이 있다. 특히 현대중국어의 품사분류를 통해 각종 품사의 어법특징과 어법기능을 중심으로 살펴본다.

Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this class is to examine various educational theories for 
learning practical Chinese as a second language with a focus on Chinese 
Grammar education for non-native speakers and to analyzes the gap between 
theory and the actualy usage of Chinese language in everyday life.  The class 
will explore the grammatical features of each word class and the grammatical 
functions through the word classification system of Modern Chinese language. 

    The evaluation will be based on the following:
    Mid-term Exam - 30%
    Final Exam - 30%
    Class Participation - 10%
    Class Attendance - 10%
    Quiz - 20%
