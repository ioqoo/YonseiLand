Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed for students who have never taken a marketing course for a full semester before. Therefore, the general objective of this course is to introduce the basic marketing concepts and functions with a special focus on the creative industries. This course will introduce the concepts and skills of contemporary marketing management during lectures and class discussions. The students will learn how to analyze complex business situations, identify underlying problems and decide on the courses of actions with the help of the modern marketing management techniques. To this end, the content-related learning goals of this course are an understanding of marketing environment, marketing information management, buying behavior, segmentation, and target market selection and the elements of marketing mix (product, pricing, advertising and promotion, and distribution channel). The students will have an opportunity to apply such concepts and skills in the team project.
