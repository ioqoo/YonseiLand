Syllabus_Korean
##
- 사회적기업(소셜벤쳐) 및 사화적기업가정신의이해

##
students can expect to 
1) Understand the basic concept and vision of social entrepreneurship. 
2) Be exposed to leading actors, social entrepreneurs and understand their roles in making changes for development and challenges facing them    3) Understand empathy enterprise and creating shared value and collaboration between the business entrepreneur and social entrepreneur 
4) Be motivated to become change-makers and given a chance to prepare an idea and execution and financing plan to solve a social problem during the course.
