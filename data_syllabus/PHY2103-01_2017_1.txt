Syllabus_Korean
##
Modern physics represented by relativistic theory and quantum mechanics 
involves various areas of physics established after early 20th century. This 
lecture provides the overview of entire areas of modern physics such as 
relativistic theory, quantum mechanics, statistical physics, solid-state 
physics and particle physics.
