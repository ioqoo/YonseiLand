##
This course introduces students to the Christian Bible and the related matters. The Bible has been a canon for two major religions (Christianity and Judaism) and a sacred text for one (Islam) and had significant influence on the history and culture of the world. In this class, students shall be trained to look at the Bible not as an object for belief or disbelief but with reason, which will facilitate conversations between people of different religious beliefs. The issues to be discussed will include the following: the meaning and scope of the Bible, the history of the formation of the Bible, the history behind the Bible, various stories in the Bible and their interpretations, and todayâ€™s ethical and cultural issues related to the Bible.
