Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides  a fundamental knowledge of concrete structures, so 
students can understand concrete behavior and design methods. This course 
focus on the structural analysis and design of concrete members; anchorage and 
serviceability in flexural members, vertical members including short and 
slender columns, and introduction of one- and two-way slabs.
