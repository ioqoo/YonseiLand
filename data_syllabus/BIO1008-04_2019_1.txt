Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is aimed at understanding the basic concepts and principles of biology from molecular and cellular to organismal and population levels, and comprehending the diversity of life on Earth through time and space. The classes in most cases will cover the contents described in the text book. There will be a companion quiz session after each teaching class. The students also will be taken practice classes in the laboratory.
