Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to introduce you six major theoretical orientations 
and several models within the marriage and family therapy field through 
readings and discussions of books, and presentations of therapy models.
Evaluation: midterm exam 30, final exam 30, summary 10, client experience 10, 
team project 10, attendance 10.
