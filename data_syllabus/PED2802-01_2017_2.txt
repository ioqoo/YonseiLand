Syllabus_English
##
This course is targeted for the athletes to learn how to appropriately apply 
the basic principle of the training and rehabilitative exercises to atheletic 
fields to prevent injuruy and to improve atheletic performance.

Once completion of this course, you will be able to:
- Understand fundamental movements in weight training
- Make and understand exercise programming
- Understand the basics of rehabilitation and sports injury
