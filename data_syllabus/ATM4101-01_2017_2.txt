Syllabus_Korean
##
대규모 및 중규모 대기순환을 결정짓는 역학 법칙들을 이해하고, 이를 각 대기변수들
의 관계를 통하여 확인한다. 또한, 대기에서 일어나는 여러 시,공간 규모의 파동 현
상을 기본 역학과 그 특성을 이해한다. 또한 파동의 발달과 관련된 불안정성 들
(instabilities)을 이해한다.

Dynamic Meteorology II

Fall 2017
Department of Atmospheric Sciences


Instructor : Hye-Yeong Chun (☎ 2123-2692)
     Science Bld. room 543
     email: chunhy@yonsei.ac.kr
Class time: Tuesday(14:00-15:50), Thursday (16:00-17:50) 
Textbook: “ An Introduction to Dynamic Meteorology " 
      Fourth or Fifth Edition by James R. Holton
Grading :  mid-term exam. (30%), final exam. (30%), assignments (20%), class HW 
(10%), project (10%)


Objective of the course: 
To understand dynamical laws determining the large-scale and mesoscale 
atmospheric circulations and the relationship between the observed 
meteorological variables.  To understand various scales of atmospheric waves 
and instabilities to maintain them.

Contents

I. Qasi-geostrophic flow system: Chapter 6
 Derivation of the quasi-geostrophic system 
 Prediction via the quasi-geostrophic equations
 The diagnosis of quasi-geostrophic vertical motion

II. Atmospheric Waves: Chapter 7.
 Gravity waves
 Rossby waves

III. Baroclinic Instability: Chapter 8
  The two-layer model
