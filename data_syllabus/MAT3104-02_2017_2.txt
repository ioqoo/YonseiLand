Syllabus_Korean
##
1변수 함수에 관한  기초 해석학을 배운다. 
다음의 4 부분을 기초부터 심도있는 영역까지 체계적으로 학습한다. 
(1) Completenss(완비성) 공리와 Cauchy Sequence,그리고 Sequence의 수렴성.
(2) Pointset topology 와 Continuity.
(3) Series of functions 와 Uniform Continuity.
(4) Calculus (Differentiation and Integration).

Syllabus_English
##
We shall cover the following 4 parts of elementary analysis:
(1) Completenss axiom and Cauchy Sequence, and Convergence of Sequences.
(2) Pointset topology and  Continuity.
(3) Series of functions and Uniform Continuity.
(4) Calculus (Differentiation and Integration). 

Grade is based on 
midterm(100)
5 Home work sets and two short quizzes ( 150)
Final exam(accumulative) (150)
