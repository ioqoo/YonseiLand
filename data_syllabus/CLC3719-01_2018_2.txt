Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will examine some major texts on European modernity together with postmodern challenge. The selections from the works of Hegel, Heidegger, Wittgenstein and others will be discussed with the critical lectures/discussion of the issues in phenomenology, epistemology and philosophy of art.
