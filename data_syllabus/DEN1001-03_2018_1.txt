- Course Description & Course Objectives
In order to adapt well to the dental school life for six years and to become 
as a desirable dentist after graduation, students will be able to learn how to 
develop proper attitude and relieve the stress.

- Grading Policy
Presentation 40%
Attendance/Attitude 40%
Self Assessment 20%
