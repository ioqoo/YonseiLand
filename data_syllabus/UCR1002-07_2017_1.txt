Syllabus_English
##
This class builds the basis for enjoying classical music. It provides the 
opportunity to directly experience and hence understand classical music by 
performing as an ensemble or an orchestra. The class primarily focuses on 
ensemble or orchestra practice, and students have to perform before mid-term 
and final-term examinations.
*Evaluation - Attendance(50%), Class attitude(40%), Assignment(10%)
