Syllabus_Korean
##
This class is focused on aiding students to pass English interviews.  
If you have taken a class with Jason before, know this class will be much more difficult.
Do not take this class unless your goal is get a job at a company.  
Do not take this class if you do not know what work is.  

Goals
Students research in depth the company they choose
Students improve on how to think quickly (not rehearsed)
Students improve their confidence while interviewing, and understanding the interview process
Encourage students critical thinking and analytical abilities
