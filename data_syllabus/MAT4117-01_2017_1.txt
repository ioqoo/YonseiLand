Syllabus_Korean
##
시간에 따라 관찰된 시계열자료를 분석하는 방법, 즉 시계열 분석을 집중적으로 강의
특히, 시간영역분석을 중점적으로 다룬다. 또 유도된 여러 결과들을 이학, 공학, 금융학, 경제학 등 다양한 분야에서 나
타나는 실제 자료에 적용해 본다. SAS(또는 SPSS 등) 통계패키지의 사용법을 숙달하고 이것을 
자료처리에 적용한다.

Syllabus_English
##
Time series analysis is concerned with data which are not independent,
but serially correlated, and where the relations between consecutive 
observations are of interest. One of its main applications is to forecasting, 
and the Box-Jenkins approach is attracting more and more attention. 
The Box-Jenkins approach deals only with the time domain and discrete data, 
and this course falls into three parts: (1) The properties  of a flexible 
class of stable linear statistical models, the ARMA class, are discussed. 
(2) The Box-Jenkins iterative cycle of Identification. (3) Estimation and 
Verification is the described for these models, and it is shown how such a 
satisfactorily fitted model can be used in forecasting. (4) Forecasting of the 
future values. Several case studies use real data to show in detail how the 
univariate Box-Jenkins method is applied. All data analysis presented in this 
course is carried out by using the statistical package SAS(Statistical 
Analysis System).

Grading: Midterm 40%, Final test(for real data analysis) 40%, Homework 15%, 
etc.
