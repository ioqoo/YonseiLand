Syllabus_Korean
##
The objective of this class is to learn the basic of the game of golf. This includes the terminology, rules, etiquette, and basic swing mechanics (putting, chipping, and golf swing).
- Introduce variability iron shots, and correct contact of the shot.
- Introduce fairway wood shot and bunker shot. 
- Identify if student have right golf club and explain how it can influence the performance.
- Find your perfect pre-shot routine to help your mind control.
- Suggest improvement strategies on their pre-motion and in-motion.
- Encourage to focus on social aspects (fun) of golf.
