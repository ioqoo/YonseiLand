Syllabus_Korean
##
The objective of this course is to assist students to be able to read and 
write the most commonly used Chinese characters in modern Korean writings. The 
topics will include the historical development of Chinese characters, the five 
principles of formation, the basic structures, the 214 radicals, methods of 
using Sino-Korean dictionaries.
