Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to: 
- Offer an overview of the basic elements that comprise effective consecutive interpreting. 
- Provide diverse training methods of interpreting and research methods for related topics. 
- Introduce various interpreting activities such as interview interpreting, presentation interpreting, and whispering.
