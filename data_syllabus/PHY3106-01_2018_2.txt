Syllabus_English
##
This course will present an introduction to an equilibrium statistical 
mechanics. Topics include thermodynamics, Helmholtz free energy, and quantum 
ideal gas. 
Evaluation: midterm; 30%, final exam; 40%, quiz; 10%, homework; 10%, 
attendance; 10%
