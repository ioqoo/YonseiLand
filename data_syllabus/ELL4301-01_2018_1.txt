Syllabus_Korean
##
Course Descriptions:
This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts and terms of statistics and explores basic statistical methods that are commonly used in linguistics studies. Topics include descriptive statistics, t-test, ANOVA, correlation, Ï‡^2 (chi-square) test, regression, linear mixed models, and basic experimental design. Students will also have hands-on experience with statistical analysis using statistical packages (mainly R (and SPSS, if available)).

Course Objectives:
* Students will development the understanding of fundamental concepts of statistics and interpret statistical results reported in linguistics studies. 
* Students will be able to apply statistical methods to real data sets using a statistical package.
* Students will be able to properly interpret statistical analysis results and report them in appropriate format.
