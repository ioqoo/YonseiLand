Syllabus_English
##
This class provides an introduction to contemporary research on the political 
economy of development. Students review the economic theory of development and 
the role assigned to political processes in the theory. Developmental state, 
welfare state, regulatory state, industrial strategies, governance, 
bureaucracy, etc will be the main themes of this lecture.
