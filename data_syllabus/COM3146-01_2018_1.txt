Syllabus_Korean
##
인터넷과 유종의 digital 인터랙티브 뉴미디어 매체의 특성과 마케팅커뮤니케이션에 활용방안
에 대한 폭넓은 이해를 토대로 새로운 마케팅과 광고전략수립의 초안을 제시하는 과
목이다. 디지털 마케팅 산업, 고객경험, 트래픽빌딩, 콘텐츠 마케팅, 효과측정, 콘텐츠 마케팅, 빅데이터 마케팅,
타게팅, 개인화등의 다양한 디지털 마케팅 전략 관련 주제를 다룬다.

Syllabus_English
##
This course provides a general overview of interactive digital new media 
communications in the context of advertising, marketing and communication.  
Most of the content will focus on digital media as an evolving 
communication and marketing channel.  Lectures and discussion will provide you 
with the understanding and skills to be critical consumers and quality users 
of Web content and other interactive media.  Course Objectives include: 
•To obtain an understanding of important issues in interactive digital 
communications and marketing
•To develop an understanding of digiral media as an evolving communication tool
•To create the knowledge of how people process and respond to interactive 
marketing messages
•To develop creative and strategic thinking about interactive marketing 
communication
