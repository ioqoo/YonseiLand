Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to survey basic phonological data and learn to analyze 
such phonological data as a linguist. Upon successful completion of this 
course, you should be able to 1) find phonological patterns from language data 
and 2) formulate rules and identify/discover constraints that explain observed 
data.
