The purpose of learning about the environmental design is to let student 

understand what the space is. We experience spaces either created or developed 

by various relationships between people and their surroundings. Therefore, 

students are to be educated about understanding the various relationships 

formed by environmental elements, such as building structure formed space as 

well as human beings. Beside this main concept, students have to find the 

method to express its inter-relationships for using graphic and rendering 

techniques clearly.
