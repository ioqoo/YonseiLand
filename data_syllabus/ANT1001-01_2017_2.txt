Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to introduce students to the anthropological study of cultural differences, and also to provide background knowledge to those who intend to major in cultural anthropology.  It surveys the basic concepts and research methods of the field and the key areas of inquiry in cultural anthropology today.  Through this course, students will be encouraged to comprehend the world as others see it, and explore social phenomena from diverse perspectives of different ethnicity, gender, class, generation, religion, and so forth.
