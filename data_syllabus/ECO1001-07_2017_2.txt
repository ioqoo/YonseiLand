Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is a introduction to concepts, such as how each economic agent
(consumers, firms and government) acts, how market is organized and works, and 
how firms compete in different structures of the market. Then see how 
government decisions or policies shape and constrain the climate of domestic 
economy and international business. Basic economic concepts and theories are 
dealt in the class. So, students are encouraged to explore what makes firms 
and nations competitive in the global economy and how competitive advantages 
are earned across national borders.
