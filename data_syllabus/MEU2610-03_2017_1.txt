Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is intended for students who are interested in Thermosciences.  
The course will begin with the first law of thermodynamics and properties of 
matter.  Control volume analysis and energy concept will be followed.  The 
second law of thermodynamics and entropy concept will be covered.  The more 
practical aspects of thermodynamics such as vapor power system, gas power 
system and thermal management system will be also presented in this course.
