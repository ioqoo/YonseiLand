Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to give students the basic knowledge on 
the vocabulary and the grammar of Korean language. 
 It emphasizes the development of basic communication skills 
through intensive exercise and practice of sample patterns of 
dialgue and basic grammar. 
 Students will engage in a variety of activities in speaking, listening, 
writing, and reading to improve their communication skills in Korean.
