Syllabus_Korean
##
의복의 제작자/마케터/머천다이저로 의류제품에 대한 총체적인 관리법을 숙지하
여 올바른 관리법을 소비자에게 제시하고, 용도에 맞는 의복을 설계할 수 있는 능력
을 함양한다.
(1) 섬유, 옷감의 특성, 의복의 구성요소 및 의류제품 종류에 따른 적절한 
    관리법을 파악하여 제품의 유용도를 유지하고 수명연장방법을 고찰한다.
(2) 다양한 세제와 세탁의 원리에 기초한 올바른 의복관리법을 학습한다.
(3) 의류제품에 관련된 소비자보호법을 알아보고 소비자피해/고발/해결사례를 
    조사하여 올바른 의복관리 기술을 제시할 수 있는 방법을 습득한다.
(4) 적절한 의복계획과 합리적 재활용방안을 모색한다.

Syllabus_English
##
Care and maintenance of garments and textile products will be discussed to put
the right labeling for consumers as an apparel maker or marketer/merchandiser. 
Practices to minimize product claims and maximize consumer protection and 
green environment are included.
This course will be performed as follows:
Students learn the requirements of government regulations and laws for apparel 
labeling and safety to communicate to consumers on textile products.
The content is comprised of four parts:
(1) Understanding the importance of and recognizing standard care symbols for
 serviceability of clothing and textile products
(2) Applying the right care and maintenance of apparel products by the effects 
of refurbishing on textiles and end products according to different laundering 
and dry cleaning methods with various detergents
(3) Improving students`s abilities to solve consumer claims and give consumers 
the right recommendations on apparel caring and maintenance by understanding 
consumer protection laws (Consumer Claims Acts) and by investigating consumer 
policy, consumer reports and cases of consumer prosecution claims and solutions
(4) Finding the way to rational reuse and recycling by a right wardrobe 
planning.
Grading(100%):Report(30%), Midterm exam(20%),Final exam(30%),and Attendance
(20%).
