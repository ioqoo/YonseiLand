Syllabus_Korean
##
In this class, students learn microeconomics theory, which is the corner stone 
of the study of economics. In particular, students learn the optimizing 
behavior of consumers and firms, and the process of determination of 
equilibrium prices in a market.

Goals:

1. Understand consumer theory
2. Understand producer theory
3. Understand three important types of market: perfect competition, monopoly 
and oligopoly
4. Understand how people make decisions under uncertainty
