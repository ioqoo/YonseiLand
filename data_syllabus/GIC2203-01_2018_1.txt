Syllabus_Korean
##
The major objective of this course is to provide a basic understanding of marketing concept and its importance in operating business. 
Marketing starts from investigating consumers` unmet needs, entails analyzing competitive market environments, identifying target consumers, 
and developing positioning, marketing mix strategy, and its implementation program. 
This course is designed to facilitate student learning by providing an opportunity to:

1. Investigate theory and practice of marketing
2. Explore how marketers in the field manage their brand and products via a guest lecture
3. Improve critical thinking and communication skills
