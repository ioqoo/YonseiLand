Syllabus_English
##
The objective of this course is to provide basic knowledge and information on 
experimentations through selective experimental works to learn mechanical, 
electrical, optical, electrochemical, microstructural & thermal, and thin film 
properties of materials. It also offers an opportunity designing materials for 
specific applications. In this semester, three optional experiments among six 
topics as listed above will be taken.
