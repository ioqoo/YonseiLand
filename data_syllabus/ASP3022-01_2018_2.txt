Syllabus_Korean
##
The aim of the course is to introduce to students a basic body of knowledge that will enable them to understand varied social phenomena relating to digital media and popular culture. To do this, this course will offer students with opportunities to familiarize themselves with concepts and issues regarding digital media. Various themes and issues will be addressed throughout the course ranging from the political, economic aspect of digital media to the relationship between digital media and everyday life.
