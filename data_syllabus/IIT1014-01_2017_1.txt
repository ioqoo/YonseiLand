Syllabus_English
##
This course covers a wide spectrum of convergence research for the  
undergraduate students of School of Integrated Technology. 
Each professor of the School of Integrated Technology will be the guest 
speaker and present the convergence research and development currently 
performed. This course is expected to to assist students to understand 
integrated technologies in various fields of Computer Science and Engineering, 
Materials Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Chemistry, etc.
