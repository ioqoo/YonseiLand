Syllabus_English
##
1.This course have two objectives:①historical review of Korean architecture
②theoretical understanding of traditional East Asian architecture through 
comparative analysis between the East and the West
2.This course will be composed in:①one hour lecture with slide show
②one hour lecture on architectural theory
③students` field survey of architectural sites
3.Students` performance will be evaluated by:①Final exam(45%)
②weekly paper(45%) ③class participation(10%)
