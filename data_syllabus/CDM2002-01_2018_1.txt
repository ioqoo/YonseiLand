Syllabus_Korean
##
This class aims to develop integrated design thinking from the perspective of sustainable design. 
Students will be encouraged to apply diverse design methods to frame the problem and creates ideas for harnessing user-generated content through participatory design. 
Especially this class focuses on sustainable textile product design that reduces consumption and waste by building a long-term relationships between users and products. 
It is to create emotionally durable and meaningful products such as scarves, bags, cushions and clothing..etc that offer the experience of emotional attachment and involvement.
