Syllabus_Korean
##
알고있는 화성(Harmony)을 실제소리(Sound)로서 인식하고 화성의 진행방식을 손으로 익힌다.
또한 숫자저음(Figuured Bass)을 읽을 수 있도록 하여 모범적인 화성진행을 배운다.
##
Syllabus_English
##
Already known harmonies are understood as real by and ideal proceeding methods of harmonies are practised with student`s hand.
The ability of reading figured bass is planned to be acquired for the students to learn proceeding of harmonies and the basic techniques to practise 
improvisation with keyboard can be also studied.
