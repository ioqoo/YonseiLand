Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides an overview of film theories as they have developed from the early days of cinema up to the present. The course aims to enable students to understand key critical concepts and approaches to film, as well as get a clear sense of how to closely read a film`s aesthetic and socio-political strategies. Students will also have opportunities to critically compare diverse paradigms of film theory in terms of their social, cultural, and political significance.
