Syllabus_Korean
##
To become a creative future business leader with global perspectives and integrity, students in this class will gain a practical understanding of how to manage a multinational company in the global marketplace. For this, the students will visit Korean MNCs doing business in Vietnam.

"This class is designated as a Social Innovation Certificate (SIC) course. The course contents and practices address social innovation and solving social problems. The Institute for Higher Education Innovation (IHEI) may collect student assignments and course contents for other uses. For more details regarding the SIC, please visit the IHEI`s website, http://ihei.yonsei.ac.kr."

Syllabus_English
##
In this course, students in the global management track (GMT) will visit Korean companies doing business in Vietnam during the winter vacaton. During and after the tour, students will carry out research projects on entry strategy for the Vietnamese market. Students are required to present the results of their projects in the end of the semester.
