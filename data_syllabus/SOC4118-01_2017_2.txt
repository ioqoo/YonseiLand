Syllabus_Korean
##
The course reviews classic theories as well as the state-of the-art research 
in the field of social stratification and inequality. The aim of this course 
is twofold and the course will be organized accordingly. For the first few 
weeks, the class will review major developments in contemporary stratification 
theory, and for the rest of the course, studies dealing with inequality in 
various subject areas including education, mobility, labor market, gender, 
culture and politics will be reviewed and discussed.
