Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is intended to provide an introduction to basic concepts in virology and viral life cycles and to cover topics of various host responses 
induced by virus infection. Lectures will be focused on in-depth molecular mechanisms for propagation of pathogenic human and animal viruses and 
how viral infection can be controlled.
