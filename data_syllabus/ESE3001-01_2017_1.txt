Syllabus_Korean
##
This class provides introduction of electric power energy engineering. 
Fundamentals of electrical engineering including circuit analysis and 
electromagnetics will be introduced in order to provide principles of electric 
power engineering. Students are expected to learn basic knowledge and 
understanding of electric power generation, transmission, and distribution at 
the end of class.
