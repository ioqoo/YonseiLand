Syllabus_Korean
##
Virology course will discuss around the features of the multiplication cycle 
shared by all viruses. How are viruses structured? What strategies do viruses 
use to enter their host cells? How do they express and replicate their 
genomes? How do they produce new virions? How do host cells respond to viral 
infection? How might the viruses have evolved?
