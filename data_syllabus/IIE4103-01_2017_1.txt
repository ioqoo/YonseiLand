Syllabus_Korean
##
We study about the fundamentals of AI and various related techiques. Student 
also can develop ability to implement them quickly based on PROLOG. Students 
are expected to design their AI systems to support their problems. All 
students are required to develop their prototype system to prove that their 
ideas are really working in this course.

Syllabus_English
##
Internet business (e-Business) today are starting to utilize most advanced AI 
technologies. This course is designed to address basic AI foundation to the 
student. Students can make themselves familiar with those technical 
foundations, and they can further learn how to implement various AI techniques 
using PROLOG.
