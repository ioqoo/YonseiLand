Syllabus_Korean
##
By analyzing key-texts of American and British nineteenth- and twentieth-century fiction, such as Herman Melville’s Bartleby (1853), Henry James’ Daisy Miller (1878), Thomas Hardy’s Jude the Obscure (1895), and William Faulkner’s Absalom, Absalom! (1934), the course will focus on the literary representation of the psyche of enigmatic characters. The deep cultural changes that occurred in Europe and America starting from the second half of the nineteenth century to the interwar period will serve as the background for our investigation.
