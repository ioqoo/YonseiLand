Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will focus on the understanding of atomic structure and chemical bonding in compounds, discuss the characteristics of materials related to chemical bonding, and learn the basic concepts of chemical reaction, reaction stoichiometry, and thermochemistry. And properties of gas, liquid, solution and solid will be studied.

Syllabus_English
##
This course will focus on the understanding of atomic structure and chemical bonding in compounds, discuss the characteristics of materials related to chemical bonding, and learn the basic concepts of chemical reaction, reaction stoichiometry, and thermochemistry. And properties of gas, liquid, solution and solid will be studied.

Evaluation: Midterm exam: 100 pts, Final exam: 100 pts, Lab: 80 pts,Homework, quiz, class participation, and etc: 100 pts
