Syllabus_Korean
##
Advanced College English 1 (listening & speaking) is an integrated two-credit course 
designed to improve academic listening and speaking - oral and aural 
competencies essential to successfully communicate in an academic environment. 
Students will be exposed to authentic listening materials and will learn to 
communicate clearly and effectively in a group setting. Students will become 
active speakers and strategic listeners.
