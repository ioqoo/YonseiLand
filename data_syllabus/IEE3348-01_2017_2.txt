Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will study the elements and management of marketing channels. For 
our purposes, any marketing channel is viewed as an inter-organizational 
system involved with the task of making of goods and services available for 
consumption by enhancing their time, place, and possession titles. The focus 
is on how institutions can effectively and efficiently transmit things of 
value from points of production to points of consumption.
