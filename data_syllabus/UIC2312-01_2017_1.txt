Syllabus_Korean
##
This course seeks to develop students` communicative skills in Japanese. 
Students are encouraged to learn how to interact in Japanese in different 
settings that reflect various life situations in Japan. This interactive 
learning process will take place along with further acquisition of grammatical 
patterns and kanji reflective of intermediate proficiency.
