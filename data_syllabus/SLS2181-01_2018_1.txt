Syllabus_English
##
By the end of this course students will be able to 
1. Maintain a rally of 10-15 shots, using a combination of backhand and forehand groundstrokes & volleys
2. Execute a legal “long serve” at least 7 of 10 tries
3. Execute a legal “short serve” at least 7 of 10 tries
4. Execute an “overhead smash” at least 5 of 10 tries
5. Demonstrate a knowledge of pickleball rules in a singles match
6. Demonstrate a knowledge of pickleball rules in a doubles match
7. Demonstrate good sportsmanship in practice and in tournament play
