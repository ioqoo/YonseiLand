Syllabus_Korean
##
The course is designed to provide students with the opportunities to perform 
independent researches on the topics of their interest. The students will be 
exposed to diverse research areas in the laboratories in the department, and 
will conduct researches by applying the knowledges acquired in the ME courses. 
Research methods, ethics, and writing/presentation skills will be taught in 
the course.
