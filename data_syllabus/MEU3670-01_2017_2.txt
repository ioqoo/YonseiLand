Syllabus_English
##
In this course, following issues are introduced:
- Dynamics and vibration of lumped-parameter and continuous models of mechanical systems.
- Free and forced vibration of linear undamped and damped lumped parameter single and two-degree of freedom models of mechanical systems.

The grades will be assigned based on the following weights:
 Exams : 85%,
 Report, Diligence (measured by presence) : 15%.
