Syllabus_Korean
##
Course Objectives:

(1) Students will acquire understanding of the basic role of markets and marketing in society.
(2) Students will learn basic marketing concepts and marketing processes.
(3) Students will be exposed to the basic practices and problems in marketing and apply     
      introductory marketing concepts to real world questions. 
(4) Students will develop critical thinking, writing, presentation, and teamwork useful for future  
      managers.
