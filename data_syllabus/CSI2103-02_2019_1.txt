Syllabus_Korean
##
The organization of data in storage affects how efficiently we can access and
update the data. Choosing appropriate data structures is therefore an essential
first step of writing efficient programs. This course introduces students to
fundamental building blocks of data structures including arrays, linked lists,
stacks, queues, trees, graphs, hash tables, and their related algorithms. Selected
advanced topics will also be covered.
