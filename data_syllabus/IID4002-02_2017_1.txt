Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to help the senior students maintain focus on their 
topics, and guide them through the design process into a tangible body of 
original design work of professional quality. Students synthesize the 
research, document the design process, and produce a final project based on 
the given main thesis, but chosen subject of their research.
