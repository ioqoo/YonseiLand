Syllabus_English
##
Introduction to data assimilation theory and techniques. The course begins 
with review of basic linear algebra, probability theories, and statistics that 
are necessary to understand data assimilation theories. The remainder of the 
course is concerned with estimation theory and various types of data 
assimilation techniques.

The final grade is determined from class attendance (5%), mid-term exam (40%),
final exam (40%), and term project (15%).
