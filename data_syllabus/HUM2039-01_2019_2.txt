Syllabus_English
##
Goal
This course deals with digital technologies and application software tools 
that are used for storing, organizing and disseminating information. 
Specifically, it focuses on introduction to programming that can process 
digital data in the Big Data Age. 

Grading
Final Exam(Mid-term, Final): 50%
assignment: 30% 
Class participation and attendance: 20%
