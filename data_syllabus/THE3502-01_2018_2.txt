Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will be an introductory course on Contemporary Ethics.  Throughout 
the term, we will examine themes and issues on contemporary moral 
philosophies, with a particular emphasis on applied ethics.  Applied ethics 
involves examining specific controversial issues.(Please refer to weekly 
topics for further information)
Some questions to be covered, "How does one go about behaving 
ethically?", "How do we define ethical behavior?"
