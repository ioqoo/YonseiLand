Syllabus_Korean
##
1. 원격교육의 개념및 역사적,이론적 배경을 알고 기술하도록 한다.

Syllabus_English
##
Course Objectives:
1.Participants will be able to define the concept of distance learning and 
depict the historical and theoretical context.
2.Participants will be able to articulate the types of distance learning and 
discuss with peer learners on the application of various methods.
3.Participants will be able to design and develop a continuous learning 
program on their topics.

Assessment: 
Grades are based on completion of course requirements and on the scope, 
quality, and creativity of the assignment(s). 
Interim project(report)-30%, fianl project(report+product)-30%, 
single presentation-20%, attendance-10%, online discussion-10%
