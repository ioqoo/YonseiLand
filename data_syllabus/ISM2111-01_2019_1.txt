Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will be an interest for students majoring in international studies, political sciences, and anyone else who is concerned over world history, international relations, foreign policy and diplomacy. The Cold War period is looked at through the lens of the predation theory that explains raising state strategy toward declining great powers (more in “Rising Titans, Falling Giants” by Shifrinson, Joshua R. Itzkowitz, Cornell University Press, 2018).
