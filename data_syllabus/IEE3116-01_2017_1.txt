Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to explore traditional and modern Korean philosophy at its 

introductory level. We are going to examine Korean tradition in comparison 

with western philosophical tradition. We will look at dominant philosophies 

and philosophers in the Korean tradition. One of aims of this class is to 

permit students to have opportunity to have cross-cultural understanding of 

Eastern and Western philosophies.This course is designed as a lecture class 

with presentation and free discussion of all participants.
