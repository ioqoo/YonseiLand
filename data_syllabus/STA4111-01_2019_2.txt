Syllabus_Korean
##
Students will learn how to price standard life insurance and annuity products. The course will be highly mathematical with quite a few actuarial formulas used in both academia and industry.
As many numerical examples are from past SoA (Society of Actuaries) exams, this course can also help students prepare for exams of actuarial professional designation of Korea and (or) North America.
