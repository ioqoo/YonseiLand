Syllabus_Korean
##
연세대학교와 인천시, 인천시교육청과 협약을 통해 진행되는 `연인 프로젝트`로, 인
천시
의 소외 계층에게 다양한 학습활동을 지원하는 멘토링 수업임. 
이 수업의 목표는 교육불평등 해소에 기여하고, 사랑과 배려를 실천하여 봉사의 책임
감과 
리더십을 함양하는 데에 있음.

Syllabus_English
##
"This class is one of thirteen(13) `YonIn Dream Mentoring Program` 
classes. `YonIn Dream Mentoring Program`, which will be operated in 
cooperation with Incheon Metropolitan City, Incheon Metropolitan Office of 
Education and Yonsei University, is a kind of community engagement & services 
to provide study mentoring, arts & physical curriculum mentoring, and club 
activities mentoring for students of elementary, middle and high schools in 
Incheon Metropolitan City.
`YonIn Dream Mentoring Program` aims to improve educational opportunities for 
relatively vulnerable students, to construct win-win relationship between 
Incheon Metropolitan City and Yonsei University, and to encourage social 
responsibility and servant leadership. "
