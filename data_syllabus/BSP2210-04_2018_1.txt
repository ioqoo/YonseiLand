Syllabus_Korean
##
타악기의 앙상블 수업을 통해 타악기의 다양성을 이해하고 고전부터 현대음악 앙상블까지의 특징과 차이점을 실내악을 통해 익히고
 이로 인해 실내악은 물론 오케스트라에서도  음악적 역량을 갖춘 연주자를 양성하는데 그 목적을 둔다.

Syllabus_English
##
By teaching various stick controls that can push the techniques to the highest limit, this class maximizes individual performers’ abilities, and through an ensemble class between percussion majors and wind instrument majors, we handle the characteristics of both classical music and modern music and the differences between them, analyze sound technique and musical harmony, and raise the ability to control them. Through these, this class raises the performers’ qualities that will enable them to exert their abilities not only in general chamber music but in orchestra.
