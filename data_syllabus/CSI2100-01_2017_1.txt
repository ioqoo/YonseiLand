Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims introduces students to the techniques, tools and thought processes that computer 
scientists apply to solve problems. The course will particularly highlight the role of computation 
for solving real-world problems. Students will be introduced to the two major abstraction principles
in computer science: algorithms and data-structures. The implementation language of this course is 
Python 3.
