Syllabus_Korean
##
Photogrammetry is the most popular solution to build geospatial information 
for GIS, which is based on conceptual framework fo surveying engineering.  In 
this class, students will learn the practical aspects and the principles of 
photogrmmetry, including the fundamentals of digital image processing, basics 
of photogrammetry, photogrammetric processes, orientation of aerial 
photograph, aero-triangulation, orthophoto production, and remote sensing.
