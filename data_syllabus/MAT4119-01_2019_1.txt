Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this course is to understand basic financial models and to 
study pricing and hedging theories in mathematical finance. The course covers  
Brownian motion, stochastic calculus, Black-Scholes formula for European 
option, pricing of exotic options, bonds and credit derivatives and etc.
