Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this Intermediate English reading and writing course is to encourage students to utilize their English knowledge to express their own thoughts and feelings in English written format.   Additionally, the course aims to assist students to further their ability to logically structure their ideas.  Note all instruction will be given in English.

Goal
Students utilize their English knowledge
Students practice expressing their thoughts and feelings in written format
Students practice planning and organizing their ideas logically
