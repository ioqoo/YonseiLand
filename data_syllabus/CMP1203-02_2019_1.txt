Syllabus_English
##
Goals of the Course
1. Focus on the diatonic chords and their syntactical usages in music literature
   (diatonic voice leading and syntactical logics of the harmonic progressions). 
2. Analyze harmonic idiom in the eighteenth century.
3. Learn formal prototypes from the phrase to the song forms in the 18th century.
