Syllabus_English
##
There are many kinds of martial arts in Korea. Only Taekyun has been designated as cultural property as a martial art by Korean government. 
 Also, UNESCO has registered Taekyun as a World Cultural Heritage.Because Taekyun is a Korean traditional unique martial art based on Korean
 philosophy and movement.
    You will be able to learn Korean unique fighting techniques and to get a healthy body.
 And you can understand Korean traditional culture and movements.
