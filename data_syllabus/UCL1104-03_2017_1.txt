Syllabus_Korean
##
This course of basketball is designed to improve your basketball playing performance and health in an academic setting. It places an emphasis on basic lectures related with basketball and playing game. This course is organized by the study of learning to know the rule of basketball correctly based on Korea University Basketball Federation; you are expected to actively engage with the instructor and other students to do various activities of basketball in person for success in this course.
