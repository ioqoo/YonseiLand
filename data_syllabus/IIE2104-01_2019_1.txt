Syllabus_Korean
##
Mathematical and algorithmic approach for the Operations Research models. 
Modeling and analysis for the manufacturing, services, public and social 
systems.  Course includes the quantitative approach and solution search 
techniques. Linear programming, transportation and assignment models, integer 
programming, dynamic programming, and network flow analysis. System 
application experiences using the commercial software as a term project.
