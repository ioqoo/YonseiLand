Syllabus_Korean
##
Microeconomics is about the decision making of consumers and firms and examines how different market mechanisms operate to allocate resources. We mainly study consumer and producer theory, theories of markets, including competitive markets and monopoly behavior.  
This course will emphasize the development of microeconomic models to analyze economic decision-making of agents. It will endow the students with the basic toolkit of microeconomic theory and prepare them to take more advanced courses of economics.
