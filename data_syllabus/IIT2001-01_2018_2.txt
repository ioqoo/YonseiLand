Syllabus_English
##
1. Course Description & Goals

This course will cover the principles of algorithms.  Topics to be focused on 
are: sorting (heapsort, quicksort), data structures (hash tables, binary 
search tree, red-black tree), dynamic programming, greedy algorithms, and 
graph algorithms.


2. Target Students

Freshmen of the School of Integrated Technology


3. Texts & References

Introduction to Algorithms (3rd edition) / Cormen, Leiserson, Rivest, and Stein


4. Course Requirements

This class requires assignments, a midterm exam, and a final exam.


5. Grading Policy

Attendance (10%), 3-Minute Reviews (10%), Assignments (10%), Midterm exam 
(30%), Final exam (40%), Optional final project
