Syllabus_Korean
##
We meet a variety of managerial issues from concept development till successful launch into the market.
This course will help students to understand those issues and design possible solutions for those issues

We will look into case studies, prototyping solutions for each issue we are facing now, and 
listing any drawbacks and risks for further consideration. 

-Students will read core articles and chapters out of top journals such as 
HBR, Gartner etc, will pick up and watch related video clips available in TED, 
YouTube etc. Students will delve into key findings and insights through hot 
discussions and will finally enjoy different views and ideas from colleagues .
