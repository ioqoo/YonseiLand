Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to introduce topics related to quality in a 
broader sense. Topics include Technological Diffusion Forecasting, Conjoint 
Analysis, QFD and Taguchi Design for Product & Process, SPC, CRM, ISO, MBNQA, 
Six Sigma, & Customer Satisfaction Index.
At the end of the semester, students are expected to have a holistic view of 
quality by understanding the methodologies applicable to not only on-line but 
also offline quality improvement.

Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed to introduce students to the quantitative issues 
related to the topics such as Technological Diffusion model, Product & Process 
Design, Conjoint analysis, SPC, Customer Satisfaction Index, Data Mining, ISO, 
MBNQA, Six Sigma & CMM.
