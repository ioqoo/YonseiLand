Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims at introducing the essentials of management and enabling 
students to apply them to organizations and their careers in a complex global 
environments.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This course consists of 5 parts;
Part 1: Management -Introducing management in terms of present-day dynamics and 
historical foundations.
Pert 2: Environment -Setting the environmental context within which today`s 
managers function.
Part 3: Planning and Controlling -Covering information and ecision making, 
planning processes and techniques, control processes and systems, and strategy 
and stratigic management.
Part 4: Organizing -Explaining the essential building blocks of organizations
Part 5: Leading -Exploring key leadership skills and competencies
