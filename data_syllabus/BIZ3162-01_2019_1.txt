Syllabus_Korean
##
The learning objective of this course is to introduce students to financial institutions, pricing of financial assets, and the mechanism of financial markets. The course also develops a series of applications of principles from economics and finance to explore the interaction between financial markets and the macroeconomy. In addition, it will focus on many public issues and examine how the central bank, one of the most important players in financial markets, conducts its operation and study the effects monetary policy on the economy.
