Syllabus_Korean
##

Syllabus_English
##
This class aims to provide students with opportunities to meet the graduates  
who are working in the field of communication. There will be a lot of 
informal  meetings with the graduates, which will make current student get 
familiar with  the professional field of communication. In addition, the class 
intends to  encourage students to seek their professions in the field of 
communication,  applying what they learned in the class to the real world 
settings.
