OBJECTIVES OF THE COURSE 
1. To gain introductory, yet practical knowledge on the building construction 
process from the project formulation phase to the construction closeout phase
2. To provide a foundation for application to real design and engineering 
problems in architecture

Syllabus_English
##
This is an advanced level building construction course. "Fundamentals of 
Building Construction" is a prerequisite. The entire course is structured to 
help teams of students develop preliminary construction plans for unbuilt 
buildings or buildings built in foreign countries through lectures, guest 
lectures, site visits, and literature survey. Students will be asked to invite 
a technical advisor who can help them develop high-quality construction plans. 
Several experts in charge of work tasks will be invited to provide high-the 
construction method on each work task with students.
