This course attempts to develop social entrepreneurs who innovatively solve social problems related to social welfare. It reviews concept of community, theoretical frameworks for understanding community, definitions and community practice & community welfare, community practice models (locality development, social planning, social action, etc.) for promoting community welfare, typical phases in promoting community welfare, special skills required for promoting community welfare, and new approaches and tasks in promoting community welfare.
