Syllabus_Korean
##
To cover the state of current affairs, English through media and the changing communication requirements for global digital citizens.  Students will already be familiar with some social network basics, but this class will seek to discover more fundamental structures that lie underneath them.  Current events and the use of news reporting, as well as discourse analysis, will hope to make students aware of and more critical of the information they receive.
