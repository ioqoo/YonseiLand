Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is about the organization and management of digital information.  We take a service-oriented approach which considers selection, 
organization, access, and preservation and we consider application areas including business, science, and cultural heritage.  Students will develop 
specific skills in the use of XML and ontologies, on designing usable systems, on setting policies for content, and on implementing content management systems.
