Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is about the decisions and actions which are prerequisite to the 
consumption of every meals.  Meals require the inputs of time, money, energy, 
personal skills and abilities in addition to the the food materials. We will 
discuss decision making and actions dealing those inputs to achieve desired 
satisfaction in the aspects of nutrition, money, time and taste.
