Syllabus_Korean
##
The main purpose of this course is to understand the fundamental concepts of strategic management, acquire the capability to analyze firms and to 
think strategically as a general manager. To achieve this purpose, we will examine theories and frameworks that have been used to analyze the 
industries, firm capabilities and competitors; design business and corporate strategy; implement strategy; and evaluate the performance outcome of 
strategy. We will also cover the practical application of the theories and frameworks.
