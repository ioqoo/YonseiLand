Syllabus_Korean
##
- Introduction to fibers, yarns, fabrics, and finishes.
- This course will provide an overview of how textile components are made and how appropriate fabric or product performance is achieved.
- Course Objectives
1. To gain a comprehensive vocabulary of textile terms.
2. To correlate fibers with their properties for end-use requirements.
3. To know the distinctions between various yarn types as well as their properties.
4. To understand how textile materials are constructed by various methods and know the end uses of these materials.
5. To be knowledgeable how textile finishing alters and adapts fabrics for their intended end uses.
