Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will examine some major texts on the philosophy of culture with special emphasis on the status of science in western tradition. The selections from the works of/on Kant, Husserl, Heidegger, Wittgenstein, Derrida, Bohr, Heisenberg, Einstein and others will be discussed.
