Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a course on the study of fundamental data structures such as lists, stacks, queues, tree/search trees, hashing, and graphs, as well as sorting algorithms. 
The goal is to teach students data structures so that they are able to utilize various data structures to maximize the efficiency of programs.
##
Syllabus_English
##
This is a course on the study of fundamental data structures such as lists, 
stacks, queues, tree/search trees, hashing, and graphs. The goal is to teach 
students data structures so that they are able to utilize various data 
structures to maximize the efficiency of programs.

This course is for the Undergraduate CS students who had taken Object-Oriented
Programming course.
