Syllabus_Korean
##
History of Israel as a small and highly developed nation, which is laid in the most unstable and violent the Middle East surroundings, is unique and is worth to be discussed and analysed. Better knowledge of Israeli society, politics, and economy, its domestic and foreign policy might be taken and a key to eliminating and resolving current conflicts, or at the very least finding further solutions for stabilising of the whole region.
This course suggests the overview and analytical insight in the regional conflicts in their historical and modern perspectives with the special attention to the modern Israeli state and society.
