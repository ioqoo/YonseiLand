Syllabus_Korean
##
This course introduces the four major classes of biomolecules: proteins, 
nucleic acids, carbohydrates, and lipids, as well as the techniques used to 
study their form and function.  The major concepts and principles covered 
include biochemical evolution, protein structure and function, RNA, DNA, the 
flow of genetic information, genes, genomes, bioinformatics, hemoglobin, 
enzyme kinetics, catalytic and regulatory strategies of enzymes, 
carbohydrates, lipids, and cell membranes.
