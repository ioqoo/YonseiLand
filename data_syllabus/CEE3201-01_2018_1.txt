Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this course is to make the student develop and enhance the knowledge of fluid mechanics and its applications and practices. The basic 
principles of fluid mechanics are introduced. The basic principles include fundamental properties of fluids, fluid statics, fluid dynamics, fluid 
kinematics, and basic equations.
