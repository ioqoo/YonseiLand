Syllabus_English
##
This course aims to help students to understand certain features of English grammar
and organize them into a grammar of English on their own. For this purpose,
lectures are geared toward encouraging students to study English grammar
with a view to deepening their understanding of language.
The grading policy`s as follows: midterm 40%, final 40%, assignment & presentation 5%,
class participation 5% and attendance 10%.
