Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will examine the development process of the Korean economy. 
Starting with the economic `miracle,` the course proceeds 
to the subsequent process of stabilization, liberalization, and 
democratization. The course will then examine the nature of the 1997 crisis, 
and then investigates to the working of the current Korean economy.
##
