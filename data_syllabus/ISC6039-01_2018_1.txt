##
The primary goal of this course is to familiarize students with international 
trade law and policy issues involved in FTAs.  Students are 
expected to be able to do the following by the end of this course.

I. Understand the elements of international trade law involved in FTA 

negotiations.

II. Evaluate the final treaty text of an FTA agreement to take advantage of 
the 

business opportunities created by an FTA agreement.


III. Appraise the demands of business sectors and incorporate theirs interests 

in FTA negotiation objectives.

IV. Evaluate FTA partner countriesâ€™ trade regulatory regimes and design 

negotiating objectives that improves market access for your country`s 

exporters and investors.

V. Evaluate the results of economic analyses of FTAs.

VI. Negotiate an FTA agreement
