Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to provide students with opportunities to understand the core 
value orientations and conceptual foundations of justice and civil leadership -
 
justice, leadership, liberties and rights, equality, democracy, solidarity and 
so on - and equip students with various approaches to put them into practice. 
Students are encouraged to accustom themselves to various interdisciplinary 
epistemologies and analytical skills in order to delve into the various 
aspects 
and dimensions of justice and civil leadership. It will also help students 
deepen their understanding of the human condition, the relationship between 
the 
individual and the community, and the interaction between the state, civil 
society, and the economy.
