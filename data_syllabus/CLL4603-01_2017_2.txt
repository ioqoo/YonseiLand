Syllabus_English
##
This course is designed for the students who want to study Chinese phonetics. 
It will present the accurate pronunciations of consonants, vowels, and tones of 
Mandarin Chinese and its phonetic system to assist students to correct their 
own pronunciations. It will also discuss the mechanisms of various sound changes
 and language contacts in Chinese. 
This course will help students acquire the proper pronunciations of standard 
Mandarin and develop their sense of language.
