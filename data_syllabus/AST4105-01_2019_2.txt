Syllabus_Korean
##
A large fraction of baryons in the universe exists in interstellar space as gas and dust (interstellar medium, ISM). With the goal of probing the properties of ISM in a range of physical and chemical states, this course will cover the following subjects. 1) The history of ISM studies; 2) properties if ISM in various phases; 3) the role of ISM in galactic eosystem; 4) the role of ISM in galaxy formation and evolution; 5) intergalactic and intra cluster 
medium.
