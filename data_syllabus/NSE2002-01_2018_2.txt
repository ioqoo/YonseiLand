Syllabus_Korean
##
This lecture is mainly a laboratory course that provides lectures on basic nanotechnology. It is meant to be an introduction to the major concepts of nanotechnology device and high-tech analysis instrument for nanoscience. One of the primary objectives of this course is to make students be familiar and comfortable with the laboratory and state-of-the-art analysis and processing tools. This includes the use of chemical / polymer precursors and a variety of nanoscience equipment for enrolled students.
