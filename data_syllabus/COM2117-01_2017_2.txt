Syllabus_Korean
##
This course reviews conceptual analyses, theoretical perspectives, and research studies that focus on the interrelationship between media and society.  The nature and characteristics of media as well as the role of media in reflecting and shaping social phenomena are examined.  Major theoretical approaches to expositing media influences and effects on society are introduced, compared, and evaluated.  Students are encouraged to develop critical and informed views of important issues relevant to the social impact of media in such areas as politics, sports, music, religion, gender/group relations, prosocial practices, violence, sexual values, and commerce.
