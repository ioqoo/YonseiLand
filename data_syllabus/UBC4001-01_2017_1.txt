Syllabus_Korean
##
A research-based thesis course that offers students the opportunity to work on a comprehensive, individual project that demonstrates biological phenomenon and/or convergence between biology and other fields.
Topic to be agreed in consultation with a supervisor.
The project will be of suitable complexity for resultts to be published for an expert audience.
##
Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this course is to develop the student`s research and problem solving skills.
The course involves the specification, development and evaluation of an individual research project on a specific topic or 
problem within the broad fields of biology and/or convergence between biology and other fields.
The student is expected to systematically plan and manage the project, and to clearly present the work.
