Syllabus_Korean
##
This Course is to enjoy the pickleball. 
By participating in the class, students can learn the rules of the pickleball and play the game.

Goal: To learn pickleball’s basic skills and improve physical fitness

1. To learn and practice how to do forehand and backhand groundstrokes, serve and volley 
2. To learn how to play pickleball game (single/double games)
3. To understand pickleball’s rules and etiquette
