Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this course is to acquire scientific knowledge about the course 
of life-span development and to apply knowledge from text and lecture to 
personal and professional life. The course requirements are exams, 
essays,class attendance, on-line class participation, and psychology 
experiment participation.
