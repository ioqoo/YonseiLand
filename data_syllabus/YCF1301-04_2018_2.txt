Syllabus_English
##
Chinese 1 is a beginner`s course of Mandarin Chinese. This course aims to help students build a fundamental capability of oral Chinese in real-life situations The main goals of the course are to improve the students’ four skills; listening, speaking, reading and writing. Furthermore, students can expect to explore the many aspects of Chinese culture. 

Method of Evaluation:
①Attendance 10% ②Mid-term exam 25% ③Final exam 25%
④Good preparation for homework assignments, class discussion, and quiz 30% 
⑤Presentation 10%
