Syllabus_Korean
##
생화학의 기본 실험법을 실습을 통해 학습한다. 특히, 현대 분자생물학의 핵심이 되
는 DNA cloning, mutagenesis, expression, purification and characterization of 
protein,RNA isolation and RT-PCR의 원리 및 실험방법을 익힌다.
##
Syllabus_English
##
Students perform experiments and learn experimental techniques.
Especially, the core skills in modern molecular biology such as DNA cloning, 
mutagenesis, expression, purification and characterization of protein, RNA 
isolation, and RT-PCR will be practiced.

Evaluation points:
Midterm exam 25%
Final exam 25%
Reports 25%
Attendance 25%
