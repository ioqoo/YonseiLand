Syllabus_English
##
An introductory course to the Korean tax laws, including basic principles common in federal taxation, individual income tax and value added tax for 
business entities.Corporate taxes are not covered in this class. Grades are based on 40% midterm exam, 40% final exam, and 20% attendance & class participation.
