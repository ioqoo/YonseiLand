Syllabus_Korean
##
This course offers the public transportation concept and design methods. It 
covers Transit route design and evaluation methods. On-hour hand-on practice 
session will be provided every week. The session will deal with transportation 
simulation packages TransCAD to analyze the public demand and public routes. 
Students will perform the term project using the simulation packages and 
present the project result in the final week.
