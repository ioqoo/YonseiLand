Syllabus_Korean
##
This course covers the basic concepts, operations, and structures
of operating systems (OS). Class objectives are:
  * Learning the basics of operating systems including processes,
    scheduling, virtual memory, multi-threading, and storage and
    I/O sub-systems.
  * Programming practices of operating systems
