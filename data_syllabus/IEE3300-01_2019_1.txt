Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a class to introduce Korean contemporary films and relevant societal 
discourses during a semester. Students are expected to learn about, after the 
semester, first, the role of film/moving images in contemporary society, 
second, the rhetoric of visual media in broader context, and lastly, Korean 
contemporary films in more concrete realm. By writing critiques on each 
subjects as well as sharing in class time, students will experience to try to 
challenge the feelings and thoughts of their own, to broaden the knowledge and 
deepen the insight on film media.
