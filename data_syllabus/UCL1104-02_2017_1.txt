Syllabus_Korean
##
This class is designed to introduce students the fundamentals of basketball, specific rules and strategies to enjoy the game. 

In addition, the students will learn essential team playing skills and etiquette through 3:3 and 5:5 game. 

This course will enable students to enjoy basketball as a recreational lifetime activity.
