##
Basic College English I is an integrated, two-credit course designed to develop fundamental English skills for students at the basic level. Student will engage in exercises to build the four core language skills: reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Students will enhance their pronunciation of sounds and conversational techniques as well as improve their listening and understanding of spoken English. They will also understand and integrate basic reading and writing skills.

Basic College English I will enable students to effectively integrate basic strategies and four skills to improve English proficiency. Students will be able to clearly identify meaningful units, key words, and major points through reading and listening practices. Through extensive exercises, students will also develop their writing and speaking skills at the sentence level.

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

- understand phrases and the highest frequency vocabulary related to areas of personal relevance

- catch the main point in short, clear and simple verbal messages and announcements

- read short and simple academic texts

- communicate in simple and direct exchanges of information on familiar topics in English

- handle short social exchanges

- write complete sentences
