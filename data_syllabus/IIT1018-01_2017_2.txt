Syllabus_Korean
##
This three credit course is designed to introduce students to the use of 
design thinking and graphic informaion design for organizing and presenting 
data and information from different sources in an interdisciplinary 
environment. 
Students will learn how to translate data to visual representations that aim 
at disseminating, documenting, and preserving knowledge in order to improve 
information communication and reception for different audiences and spaces.
