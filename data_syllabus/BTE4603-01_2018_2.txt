Syllabus_Korean
##
현대 생물학은 대용량의 데이터를 생산하고 분석하여 새로운 가설과 발견을 진행하는 데이터중심의 생물학으로 진화하고 있다. 이러한 패러다임 변화의 중심에는 유전체의 기능을 통합적으로 연구하는 기능유전체학이 위치하고 있다. 

본 수업은 다음에 열거된 다양한 기능유전체학의 개념과 최신신 연구기법, 그리고 주요 성과등을 중심으로 진행된다.

1. Introduction of Omics and data-driven biology
2. Genome Projects
3. Genomics and Transcriptomics by DNA-chip technology
4. Genomics and Transcriptomics by next-generation sequencing technology
5. Proteomics by mass spectrometry
6. Interactomics (molecular interactions)
7. Variomics (human genetic variation, genotype-to-phenotype)
8. Pharmacogenomics
9. Epigenomics
10. Regulomics
11. Microbiomics
12. Single Cell Genomics 
13. Cancer Genomics

Syllabus_English
##
It is the era of data driven science and biology is not an exception. We will learn about concepts of data-driven biology, platform technologies and their applications in genomics, transcriptomics, proteomics, interactomics, and other omics biology. Then, we will discuss about omics-inspired human disease research and medicine. Major topics of the course are the followings.
1. Introduction of Omics and data-driven biology
2. Genome Projects
3. Genomics and Transcriptomics by DNA-chip technology
4. Genomics and Transcriptomics by next-generation sequencing technology
5. Proteomics by mass spectrometry
6. Interactomics (molecular interactions)
7. Variomics (human genetic variation, genotype-to-phenotype)
8. Pharmacogenomics
9. Epigenomics
10. Regulomics
11. Microbiomics
12. Single Cell Genomics 
13. Cancer Genomics
