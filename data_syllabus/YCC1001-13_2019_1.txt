Syllabus_Korean
##
Confidence via competence is the primary goal of this course.  This is manifested through clear, cohesive speaking with solid pronunciation, appropriate intonation/word stress/pace, and active listening; similarly, these skills are supported by critical reading and concise, well-organized, and original writing.
