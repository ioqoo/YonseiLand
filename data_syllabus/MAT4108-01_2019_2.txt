Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of the course is to introduce students to the basic concepts of 
Homological Algebra : 
Modules,Diagrams, Functors, Homology of Complexes, Tensor Products, Functors 
Torn and Functors Extn 
The grading is based on homework(200%) and review tests(100%) in the middle and
at the end of the class. The main text will be
Ts. Hu, Introduction to Homological Algebra, Holden Day, 1968
