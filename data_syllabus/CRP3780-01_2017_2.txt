Syllabus_English
##
The objective of this course is to provide students with a basic knowledge of domestic legal systems/issues related to urban planning. The course mainly deals with urban planning law and urban development law.
Attendence: 10%; Assignment: 20%; Mid-term Exam: 35%, Final Exam: 35%
