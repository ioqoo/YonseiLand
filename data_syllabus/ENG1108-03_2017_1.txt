Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides freshmen students with basic algorithm development and 
problem solving methods by introducing data types, arrays, programming control 
structures, functions and mathematical tools. In addition, various information 
manipulation skills for Internet searching and multimedia data handling are 
practiced to improve the ability of utilizing computer tools in collecting, 
organizing and presenting Engineering-related subjects.
