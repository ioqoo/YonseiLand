Syllabus_Korean
##
College English II is a mandatory course for first year students. There will be a lot of writing  activities, and the readings can be challenging. The course focuses on English used in academic writing and speeches. Formal language and structure will be emphasized in developing essay content.  There will also be a final essay and speech that will be done in a group or with a partner.
