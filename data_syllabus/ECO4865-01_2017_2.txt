Syllabus_Korean
##
This course aims to understand basic concepts in real estate finance. It covers basic concepts in mortgages, time value of money, income producing property taxes, and real estate investment and accounting issues. In addition, I will cover current issues in real estate finance, such as household debt or sub-prime crisis in the US. At the end of this lecture, I will briefly introduce how to manage micro-level data and calculate some statistical moments.
