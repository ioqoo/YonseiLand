Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to introduce the fundamental skills, rules and 
strategies of Kumdo. The continuous stroke improves cardio respiratory 
endurance, muscular endurance, and agility. During combiantion strokes, the 
students will learn essential movement for sparring. This course will enable 
students to enjoy kumdon as a recreational lifetime activity.
