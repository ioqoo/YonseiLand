Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will cover introduction to human-computer interaction (HCI) theory 
and techniques as applied to the domain of general computing interfaces. The 
mobile interface issue will be also discussed in class. Teaching the various 
design techniques to build User centered computer Interface such as low-fi 
design, evaluation techniques, and building rapid proto type design.
