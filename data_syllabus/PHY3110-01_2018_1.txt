Syllabus_English
##
We study Newton`s laws for particles and system using mathmatical tools. First 
starting with vectors and matrices, we learn motion of single particles with 
examples including simple harmonic motion. Then discussion about many 
particles  follows which include topic of rocket propulsion. The semester 
concludes with  Lagrange`s formalism for equation of motions.
