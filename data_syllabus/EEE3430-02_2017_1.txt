Syllabus_Korean
##
This course provides an introduction to the structures and the characteristics 
of communication systems. This course investigates the modulation and 
demodulation techniques including Amplitude Modulation, Frequency Modulation, 
Pulse Modulation, etc and analyzes their performances.
Also, this course introduces the fundamental theory of digital communication 
systems.
