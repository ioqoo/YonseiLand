Syllabus_Korean
##
Wearable electronics is a technology for fabricating electronic smart sensors and wireless circuits, as Internet of Things (IoT), on mechanically soft forms using rigid and soft materials. This course provides an introduction to recent trends in stretchable and wearable electronic devices, including displays and smartphones, and the physics of soft, elastic materials for the wearable electronics.
