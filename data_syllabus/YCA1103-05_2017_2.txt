Syllabus_Korean
##
The goal of this course is to help students understand development of 
Christianity, the meaning of Bible and what the alternative 
leadership in our Globalised World means. We will try to learn some models 
from the Bible and to practice in our seminar in order to apply for our lives.
-To learn his/herstory of Christianity
-To explore alternative roll model of wo/men in the Bible
-To develop alternative leadership in our own context
