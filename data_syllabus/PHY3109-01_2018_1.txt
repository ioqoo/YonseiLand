Syllabus_Korean
##
1. To understand numerical and graphic analysis for basic problems in 
Classical mechanics, Electrodynamics, and Quantum mechanics using MATLAB.

2. Develop MATLAB programming skills for sicentic and engineering numerical 
analysis and technical skills for technical English oral presentations.


1.  역학, 전자기학, 양자역학 등 물리학의 기본적 영역에서 
    물리적 문제에 대한 수치 해석을 MATLAB을 사용하여 공부하고
    그 solution을 graphic적으로 이해하는 것을 목표로 함.
2.  과학및 공학의 연구진에 의해 매우 폭넓게 사용되는
    MATLAB를 활용할 수 있도록 하고 실제 물리문제를 풀어
    영어로 발표하는 능력을 기르는 것을 목표로 함.

Syllabus_English
##
The course is designed for senior undergraduate students in physical sciences 
and engineering majors to help them establish numerical analysis skills using 
MATLAB over various physical problems. Students are exposed in English 
speaking classes by both teacher`s lectures and students` oral presentation.

In the class, various numerical analysis will be covered using MATLAB routines 
along with examples and applications in physical systems.

The evaluation will be based on individual oral presentation (twice, 20% per 
presentation)
