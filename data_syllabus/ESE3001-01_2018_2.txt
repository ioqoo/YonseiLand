Syllabus_English
##
This course provides fundamental materials science and engineering. Then, it deals with renewable energy resources (e.g. solar cells, fuel cells) and energy harvesting systems (e.g. thermoelectric, piezoelectric), with a scientific examination of the energy field and an emphasis on the related materials. The course covers not only basic theories for energy materials and devices, but also practical applications.
