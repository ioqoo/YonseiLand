Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a survey course on operations management in manufacturing and service organizations.  Operations management mainly deals with the processes of value creation and delivery for customer satisfaction, and plays a key role in sustaining competitive advantage in dynamic marketplaces. 

This course aims to help students to understand various strategic and tactical decisions in the field of operations management, and introduce basics of 
supply chain management. Specifically, after completing this course, students will be able to: 
 1. Understand basic concepts and business decisions in operations management
 2. Learn cross-functional implications of various OM issues
 3. Obtain analytical techniques and relevant computer skills
 4. Gain insights on real-world operations systems
 5. Develop team management skills and leadership
