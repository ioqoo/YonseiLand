##

Syllabus_English
##
A seminar course on Aleksandr Pushkin. His major works of all genres will be read and discussed within the context of Russian Romanticism. Reading in Russian and Korean. Russia`s Pushkin myth - its making and limitations - will be critically overviewed, and each student will eventually learn to write his/her own essay of "My Pushkin." Students submit short essays on individual topics through YSCEC, and discuss and share his/her ideas with others in the classroom. Final grade is based on submitted essays, final paper, and in-class performance.
