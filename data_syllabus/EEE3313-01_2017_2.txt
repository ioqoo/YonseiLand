Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will help the students to acquire the basic knowledge 
systematically on the electronic circuits and microprocessor through the 
experiments. The students have to carry on experiments and also design 
projects during the course.

Any academic dishonest acts including cheating and plagiarism will be reported 
to mentor professor and the student affair committee in EEE Dept.
(시험 중 부정행위, 과제물 표절 등 일체의 부정행위은 학부 지도교수와 학부 학사 
위원회에 통보됩니다) 1회 적발시 해당과목 F.
