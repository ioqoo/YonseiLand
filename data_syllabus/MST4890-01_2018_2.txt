Syllabus_English
##
This course will provide the students with the state-of-the-art knowledge on nanomaterials as well as the fabrication techniques. Particular emphasis will be placed on the synthesis of 0-, 1-, 2-, and 3-diemnsional nanomaterials. Examples of advanced practical applications of nanomaterials will include various functional devices such as display, energy, and electronic systems.
