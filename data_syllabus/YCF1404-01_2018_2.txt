Syllabus_Korean
##
This is a 4 skills course integrating listening, speaking, reading and writing. Students will examine social challenges and possible solutions that will be a result of global warming and population growth. We will not be studying climate change per se, Examples of these challenges are sustainable government programs for a growing and aging society, health and care issues, education, housing and food, and whatever else students can think of. Students will work in groups of diverse majors to find different perspectives and approaches to solutions.
