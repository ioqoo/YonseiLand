Course Description
Laboratory oriented instruction to develop clinical knowledge and application 
in the evaluation of athletic injuries. This course is designed to provided 
hands-on application of knowledge in a controlled setting.

Course Goals 
Create a positive and respectful learning environment in order to increase 
knowledge in conducting a comprehensive injury examination, determining a list 
of differential diagnoses and making a clinical judgement. The student will 
integrate classroom knowledge and current evidence based research to transfer 
this knowledge into athletic training clinical practice.
