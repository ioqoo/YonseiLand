Syllabus_Korean
##
사회복지에 있어서 사회보험은 전체 국민에 대한 보편적 개념으로서 복지증진에 중요
한 역할이 부각되고 있는 만큼, 이에 대한 각 제도의 분석과 문제점을 밝히고 발전방
향 및 개선방안을 모색하고자 한다.

Syllabus_English
##
Social insurance is a general concept involving all citizens, hence its role 
is fundamental for the welfare promotion in the social welfare field. To this 
respect, this course intends to attempt an analysis of each social insurance 
system and its shortcomings, directions of development, as well as suggestions 
for improvement. Criteria for determining final marks are as follows. 
Attendance:20%, mid-term exam:30%, presentations and discussion:20%, final 
exam:30%.
