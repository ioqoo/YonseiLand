Syllabus_Korean
##
This course studies the cellular initiation and construction of organisms.  
The major models of developmental biology: snails, nematodes, Drosophila, sea 
urchins, tunicates, amphibians, fish, birds, and mammals are used to 
facilitate the study of fertilization, axis specification, cellular 
specification, gastrulation, organogenesis, limb formation, sex determination, 
and germ cells.
