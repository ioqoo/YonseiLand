Syllabus_English
##
The concepts of Calculus are extremely useful in physics, economics and 
social sciences, natural sciences, and engineering. 
This is an introduction to basic calculus with emphasis on applications to 
business, economics, management, information science, and related fields. 
Topics include relations and functions, limits, continuity, derivatives, 
techniques of differentiation, chain rule, applications of differentiation, 
antiderivatives, the definite integral, the fundamental theorem of calculus, 
and applications of integration

Grade: 
 Midterm 100,  Final 120  Quiz-test and etc 30

Text: 
Stewart - Calculus - Early Transcendentals 7th edition
