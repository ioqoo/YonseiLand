Syllabus_English
##
Organ Class (1) is designed for students to learn the basic skills as a church 
organist. 
These entail hymn playing, choir accompaniment and playing other special music 
including voluntaries. Students also learn about liturgy, organ 
building and performance practice on the music from different periods and 
countries.
