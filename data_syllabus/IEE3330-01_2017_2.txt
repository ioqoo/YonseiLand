Syllabus_Korean
##
The course is structured for students to attain the conceptual framework of international business management by exposure to various business theories, empirical findings, research analyses and case studies. To achieve these objectives, students will learn:
1. Differences in the political and socio-economic system and culture.
2. Economics and politics in international trade and investment.
3. Functions and forms of global monetary system.
4. Strategies and structures of international business.
5. Roles, functions and structure of international organizations.
6. International marketing, financing, accounting and logistics
