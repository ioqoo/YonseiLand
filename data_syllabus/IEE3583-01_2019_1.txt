Syllabus_Korean
##
Humans are social creatures. In this course, we will discuss how social we are. The primary goal of this course is to understand the fundamentals of human behavior in social context. We will cover some selected topics in social psychology as to how people think, feel, and behave in social situation.

***This class is designated as a Social Innovation Certificate (SIC) course. The students will be working on a social innovation/social problems solving project.  This is a group project which will replace the final exam. ***
