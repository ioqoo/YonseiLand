##

Syllabus_English
##
This courses examines a variety of literary theories that have developed since 
early twentieth century--formalism, new criticism, structuralism, post-
structuralism, psychoanlytical criticism, feminism, marxism, new historicism, 
and post-colonialism--and the examples of practical criticism from their 
perspectives. Mid-term and final exams, two short essays, and one group 
presentation are required.
