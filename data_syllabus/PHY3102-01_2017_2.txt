Syllabus_Korean
##
양자역학의 기본 지식을 바탕으로, 고체 띠이론(Kronig-Penney model), 시간 비의존 섭동이론, 변분법, 시간 의존 섭동이론,

##
PHY3102-01 Quantum Mechanics (2)

Fall, 2017

Classes: 3 hrs of lectures: Tue 2,3 Thu 2 (Thu 3)
Lecturer: Kyungsun Moon (kmoon@yonsei.ac.kr) 
Office: Science Hall 342 

Text: 
D. J. Griffiths, "Introduction to Quantum Mechanics", Prentice Hall (1994)

Supplementary texts: 
S. Gasiorowicz, "Quantum Physics", John Wiley & Sons
R. L. Liboff, "Introductory Quantum Mechanics", Addison-Wesley

Grading: Midterm exam:  100 %
         Final Exam:    100 %
         Homework:       40 %
         Quiz:           20 %

Course Objectives:

Band structure based on Kronig-Penney model, time-independent perturbation theory, variational 
method, time-dependent perturbation theory, adiabatic approximation, Berry phase, scattering theory etc.
