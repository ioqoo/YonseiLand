Syllabus_Korean
##
COURSE DESCRIPTION
Introduction to the chemical, biological, physical properties of food 
components and the effects of preparation, processing, and preservation on the 
finished food products.

OBJECTIVES
1. To understand the chemical, biological, and physical characteristics of 
food components and ingredients used in the preparation of foods 
2. To understand the functions of food ingredients and their interaction in 
the preparation of food.
3. To understands the basic principles and concepts involved in the 
preparation, processing and storage of food and food products
4. To be able to use food science literature and information resources and to 
apply the working knowledge to the food preparation.
