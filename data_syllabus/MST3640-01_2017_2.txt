Syllabus_English
##
Professor Kim`s lecture seeks to establish the scientific underpinnings for technologies used in electrochemical energy storage systems that make 
efficient use of energy and electrochemical processing of materials in an environmentally sound manner. 
Specific topics include electrochemical energy storage systems such as lithium secondary batteries, supercapacitors. Related to these are physical chemistry and electrochemistry of metal oxides, and nanoelectrochemistry.
