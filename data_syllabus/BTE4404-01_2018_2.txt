Syllabus_English
##
The purpose of this lecture is to
- teach basic science for Computer Aided Molecular Design (CAMD) briefly
- introduce CAMD techniques using user friendly developed s/w
- predict biological activity of small organic and biological olecules
- determine structure and function of biological molecules, especially, 
enzymes and receptors
- perform project with the problem that selected by the student of the class
