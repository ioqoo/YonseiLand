Syllabus_Korean
##
This course will cover the basic concept and techniques of financial 
economics. The principal objective of the course is to help students 
understand financial theories and develop their ability to set up practical 
financing policies and make important financing decisions to enhance the value 
of a firm. Students will learn how to make efficient investment and financing 
decision.
