Syllabus_Korean
##
Objective of this class is to provide fundamental theory of digital control 
and its applications of the digital control systems. 
Especially, we will concentrate on the modeling, analysis, and design of 
digital control systems. Detailed syllabus will be handed out in the first 
class.
