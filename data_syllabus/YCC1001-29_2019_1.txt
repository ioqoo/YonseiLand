Syllabus_Korean
##
Basic College English I will enable students to effectively integrate basic strategies and four skills to improve English proficiency. Students will be able to clearly identify meaningful units, key words, and major points through reading and listening practices. Through extensive exercises, students will also develop their writing and speaking skills at the sentence level.
Students will be able to:

Listening     Speaking
1. identify meaningful units   1. speak with intelligible
      pronunciation and intonation

2. recognize key words and major points 2. speak grammatically acceptable
      sentences

3. identify the situation and context  3. engage in basic conversation


Reading     Writing
1. locate key words from the context  1. write grammatically acceptable 
      sentences

2. understand the topic and specific   2. apply useful expressions to a 
information     new context
