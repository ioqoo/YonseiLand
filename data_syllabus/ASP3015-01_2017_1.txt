Syllabus_Korean
##
This course is designed to help the students understand the context of `media art` production in Korean society. We will study the capacity and the possibility of media art production, as a media practice and a part of contemporary arts in a society through the semester.  In particular, the class pays attention to ethnographic practice and research which has been growing rapidly in diverse areas including contemporary art, anthropology, information science, and others. During a semester, through both theory and studio sections, we throw the further questions together to make ourselves get involved dynamically in the flow Main contents of the class will include (1) the legacy of ‘media art’ in Korean society, its historical flow and uniqueness (2) common grounds 3) searching for the vision and relevant questions.  Class will be composed of three sections; lecture, discussion, and studio. Moreover, we will think of the value of ‘publicness’ in contemporary researches and art practices through each sections. Challenges and shares are encouraged most.
