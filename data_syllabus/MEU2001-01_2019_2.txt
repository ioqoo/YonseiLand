Syllabus_English
##
This course introduces core technology used in precision manufacturing systems such as a photo-lithography machine. Topics may include the overview of a photo-lithography semiconductor manufacturing machine, high-accuracy control technology, mangetic levitation technology, and high-throughput high-acceleration low-noise motor technology.
