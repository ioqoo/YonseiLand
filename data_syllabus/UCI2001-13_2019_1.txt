1. Overview
- This course provides an opportunity for UIC students to render voluntary tutoring on Yonsei International Campus.
- Tutoring is limited to the following UIC courses: MAT1002, PHY1002, CHE1002, BIO1002. 
- Students who successfully completed this course requirements will receive one social service credit. A certificate (in Korean or English) will also 
be issued at the end of the semester.
- Students chosen to serve as tutors will be enlisted to the course during the drop and add Period. 

2. Goals
The Peer Tutoring Program aims to provide quality, course-specific academic support to ISED & LSBT students at no cost. Tutoring sessions are 
tailored to aid the studentsâ€™ learning and to attend to their individual needs and concerns. Ideal tutors are those who have excelled in the course, well familiar with the course content, and have commendable study skills and habits.
 
They are expected to:
- Provide academic support to an assigned UIC freshman through tutoring 
- Assist the assigned tutee in adapting to college life
- Cultivate a spirit for service and sharing
