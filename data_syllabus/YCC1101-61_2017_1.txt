Syllabus_Korean
##
College English is a mandatory course for first year students. It will provide a systematic and thorough coverage of basic language structures and skills, through the medium of interesting and relevant material. The course focuses on English used in a variety of cultural contexts.  Each unit is divided into various sections, each with one or two main teaching points, which may be grammatical, functional, or lexical.
